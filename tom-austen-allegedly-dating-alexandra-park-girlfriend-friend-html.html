    We are all familiar with the famous Daimon Helstrom of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). However, the actor who portrayed the character of Daimon- Tom Austen, has been stealing hearts long before starring in the Hulu series Helstrom. 

    His past TV series The Royals has been the talk of the town for a very long time. However, the talks are not primarily based on his acting credentials as the fans are more interested on his relationship with his co-star Alexandra Park. 

    Tom Austen and Alexandra Park posing for a selfie in May 2019 (Photo:- Tom Austen's Instagram)

    Are Tom Austen And Alexandra Park A Thing? 

    Austen and Alexandra have undeniable on-screen chemistry, which has made fans ship the two for a very long time. Even the alleged couples made it seem like they were together off-screen by continuously sharing pictures of each other on both of their social media. However, it is quite sad for Tom and Alexandra's shippers that the two are just best buds off the screen. 

    Even though they are not together romantically, they are best friends and love each other just the same. So while the Royals fans hoped the pair to get married, Tom and Alexandra are relishing their much-hyped "friendship."

    Still Supports And Loves His Best Bud Throughout The Years 

    Although their show The Royals ended in 2018, the on-screen love interests are still best friends to date and do not back away from posting about each other on their social media. 

    On May 20th, 2021, Tom took to Instagram and shared a picture of Alexandra holding a book. As it turns out, Park came out with a book titled- "Sugar High." Austen expressed his love and support towards his best mate through his caption and included a link to the book on his Instagram bio. 

    Similarly, on Alexandra's birthday, Tom posted a picture alongside Park and her mother and continued to show his appreciation towards her. 

    Engaged To A Partner? 

    Although the on-screen couple mentioned each other as buds, fans still considered Park and Austen as a couple. However, the rumors and speculations have died after Park introduced her boyfriend, James Lafferty, to the world. 

    Also, surprise because the two are actually engaged!! 

    On September 20th, 2020, James posted a picture with Alexandra hugging him from behind while flaunting the beautiful ring, and the caption read- 

    "She said yeah..." 

    On the other hand, Tom has not spilled a single bean about his girlfriend or a potential wife. As the only girl that appears on the actor's Instagram is his best friend, fans often assume Austen to be gay. However, he has not mentioned anything about his sexuality anywhere, so the fact about his sexual orientation is quite under wraps for now.
