    NYC Jazz Club Iridium Moving

    .inarticle_banner_top { width: 728px; height: 90px; } @media(max-width: 800px) { .inarticle_banner_top { width: 300px; height: 250px; } } @media(min-width: 800px) { .inarticle_banner_top { width: 728px; height: 90px; } }

    07 Feb 2024, 00:00

    The Iridium jazz club, currently located at Broadway and 63rd Street (in the Hotel Empire basement) in New York City, is moving to 1650 Broadway (51st Street) effective Aug. 15 with Les Paul and His Trio. There will be a preview of the new club on July 30 with Paul.

    It once again will be a subterranean club at the site of Ellen's Stardust Diner. The new club will feature a Meyers Sound system as well as upgraded lighting, acoustics and sightlines.
