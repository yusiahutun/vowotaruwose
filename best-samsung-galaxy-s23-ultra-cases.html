    FAQ

    Q: Do Galaxy S22 Ultra Cases Fit on the S23 Ultra?

    Despite having the same shape and almost identical dimensions, the Galaxy S23 Ultra is slightly bigger, and the camera lenses have been moved slightly downward, meaning cases designed for the Galaxy S22 Ultra will not fit the Galaxy S23 Ultra.

    Q: Is S23 Ultra Waterproof?

    The Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra features an IP68 water-resistant certification rating. The S23 Ultra can survive slight exposure to liquids, and you can even submerge it up to a depth of 1.5 meters for 30 minutes without suffering any water damage, but that doesn't mean the device is fully waterproof. It's still recommended to keep it safe and dry.

    Q: Does the Samsung S23 Ultra Need a Case?

    Like most phones, it's a good idea to protect the S23 Ultra with a good case. The S23 Series devices feature a tough and durable armored aluminum frame, but it only takes one accidental drop on a hard surface to crack the glass at the back or, even worse, the front display.

    A good protective case can protect your new Galaxy S23 Ultra from drops, scratches, and dirt, keeping it in pristine condition for longer.
