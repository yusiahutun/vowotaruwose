    A 53-year-old woman has taken to TikTok to reveal she has no plans to stop wearing skimpy clothes in her sixth decade.

    US-based Jojo, known on the platform as @momlife1969, often shares videos depicting what it looks and feels like to be her age.

    In a recent clip, she filmed herself strolling down a beach, with the words 'Yeah this is 53' on the screen.

    As she walks along the shore, Jojo wears a snakeskin print bikini, paired with chic oversized sunglasses.

    Her long flowing brown locks are left loose, and she accessorises the beachy ensemble with a large pair of gold hoop earrings.

    The TikTok user shared a video of herself strolling along the beach, in which she revealed her age to be 53 

    Captioning the video, she wrote: 'And happy #olderwomen #cougarsoftiktok #havingfun #confident #wearthebikini #beach #slay #fyp.'

    Meanwhile, lyrics from the Pussycat Dolls' song Buttons, saying 'I'm a sexy mama', play in the background.

    Viewers took to the comments section of the video to share their thoughts on the video.

    And from the comments, it appears that most people agree with the TikTok user that she looks good, and that her age is optimum.

    One wrote: '53 is awesome!'

    Another added: 'And the award for absolutely gorgeous goes to...'

    A third wrote: 'Jo jo you look amazing doll.'

    And another chimed into say: 'Beautiful 53 Too !!' 

    Another video created by the TikTokker shows a photo of herself posing while wearing a white t-shirt and pair of skimpy black shorts 

    Also referencing her age, another said: '53 and fabulous.'

    In a similar vein, a further viewer added: 'I like 53!'

    In another video, the 53-year-old shared a photo of herself in another stylish outfit, pairing a white t-shirt with black shorts.

    Captioning the clip, she wrote: '#age is just a number #olderwomen #cougar #momsoftiktok #confident #trending #fyp #fortlauderdale.'

    The comments section of the video was full of positive feedback from people who agree that the TikTokker looks fantastic

    Once again, TikTok users took to the comments section to share their appreciation for the self-described 'cougar'. 

    Among the compliments shared by users, one said: 'always beautiful and that smile.'

    Another added: 'Mesmerizing as always.' 

    Referencing her age, a third wrote: '53 and you look amazing.'

    And another simply said: 'You are so beautiful.'
