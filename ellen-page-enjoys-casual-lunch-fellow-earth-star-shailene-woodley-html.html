    They are both down-to-earth stars who found fame at a young age.

    So it stands to reason that Ellen Page and Shailene Woodley have a lot in common.

    While the pair are not known to be friends, they certainly seemed close after enjoying lunch together on Friday.

    Catching up: Shailene Woodley (L) and Ellen Page were seen leaving West Hollywood together on Friday

    The actresses both looked laid-back and were make-up free as they left Palihouse in West Hollywood.

    Ellen led the way, clad in a red plaid shirt under a black bomber jacket, along with relaxed jeans and brown boots.

    The Juno star, 27, accessorised with a green beanie hat atop her dark locks and oversized aviator sunglasses.

    Nice to see you: Ellen led the way as the pair left Palihouse after having lunch with a mutual friends

    Common ground: While they are not known to be friends the actresses are both famously down-to-earth and found fame at a young age

    Shailene emerged just behind her pal, and strolled beside a mutual friend.

    The Descendents star was sporting a white printed T-shirt with black cropped leggings and a grey jumper tied around his waist.

    The 22-year-old also had a brown headband over her pixie cut showed what a free spirit she is by sporting what looked like black wetsuit socks instead of shoes.

    Keeping it casual: Juno star Ellen wore a plaid shirt under a bomber jacket, jeans, boots and a beanie

    Fancy footwear: Shailene wore some very interesting wetsuit socks with her cropped leggings and T-shirt

    Shailene wore a couple of pendant necklaces, several rings, an anklet, and had a black satchel slung across her body.

    The rising star is about to become a household name as the star of Divergent, the film adaptation of the bestselling book.

    But Shailene is determined that stardom won’t change her, and recently revealed how she stays grounded.

    Bare-faced chic: The 22-year-old also went make-up free and wore a brown headband atop her brunette pixie cut

    Rising star: Shailene is about to become a household name after landing the starring role in Divergent but insists fame won't change her

    ‘I’m not a big technology person. I don’t even have a smartphone. I don’t even have a cellphone!’ she told The Daily Beast.

    ‘And if I were to have one, it would be a flip-phone. There’s a bigger lack of privacy than there’s ever been, but there’s also a bigger lack of camaraderie and community than there’s ever been.’

    She added: ‘I mean … just asking people for directions. Since I got rid of my phone, having to pull over and be like, “Hey, buddy—do you know how to get here?”

    ‘I’m talking to people more than I’ve ever talked to in my life because I no longer have that crutch. The more you get away from all the technological buzz, the more freedom you have.’

    Jet setter: Shailene was spotted arriving into LAX the day before after a trip to Canada to promote her new film
