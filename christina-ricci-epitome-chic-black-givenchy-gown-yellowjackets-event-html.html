    11 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Christina Ricci epitomized chic style on Saturday night when she attended an event for the second season of Yellowjackets in a black Givenchy gown. 

    The veteran actress, 43 – who is also a 'cool mom' to her children Freddie and Cleo – stole the spotlight with her form-fitting design. 

    Ricci's dress was sleeved to the elbow and featured a high turtleneck. 

    The upper half of her gown was textured while the lower half was a ruched leather skirt. 

    The Wednesday star amplified her gothic glamour with crimson lips and gorgeous jewelry.

    Goth queen: The Wednesday star amplified her gothic glamour with crimson lips and gorgeous jewelry

    Christina posted a few snaps of her look on Instagram and gave a shoutout to Givenchy in her caption, writing: 'For your consideration this amazing @givenchy dress by @mathewmwilliams for the FYC event for @yellowjackets @showtime.'

    Showtime's Yellowjackets has received critical acclaim and a devote following from viewers and fans since the first season premiered in November 2021. 

    The second season began airing earlier this year in March, with a new episode dropping every Friday. The plot follows the Yellowjackets, a soccer team that was flying to Seattle but crashed in Canada in 1996. 

    The survivors then had to learn to survive in the wilderness, with episodes jumping to the future to show the adult version of the characters who are still coping from the trauma. 

    According to an official synopsis, the premise of season two follows: 'The Yellowjackets barely made it through summer in the woods, but now as winter begins to bite, we’ll see if hunger and desperation turn into full-on psychosis.'

    The second season contains nine episodes and will soon come to an end later this month. Yellowjackets has already been renewed for a third season.
