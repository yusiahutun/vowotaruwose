    All in One Online Messenger With A Built-In Browser

    29 Aug 2024, 00:00

    How would you like to browse the web while chat with your friends at the same time without having to leave the chatroom or change webpages? Fortunately, Chatab is an all in one online messenger with a built-in browser that lets you chat and browse at the same time.

    Similar to tools like Meebo, Chatab connects you to various IM protocols so you can chat with your friends in one page while you surf. You can register for a Chatab account where you can chat with only your Chatab contacts. You can also connect directly using your preferred IM handles to talk with your friends. Users have access to a good number of portals such as Facebook, MSN, Yahoo, Gtalk, Gadu-Gadu, Myspace, AIM, LiveJournal, QQ, ICQ, and XMPP.

    Chatab is also perfect for web office workers whose job requires them to chat with their clients while browsing the net to research their client's queries. Needless to say, using Chatab is like hitting two birds with one stone - you chat and browse at the same time.

    Features:

    • Quick chat to friends while browsing the net.
    • Tabbed browsing available.
    • Access to instant messaging anytime, anywhere.
    • Provides a vast number of chat portals such as Facebook, Yahoo, GTalk, MySpace, IRC and more.
    • Select your IM service for instant login.
