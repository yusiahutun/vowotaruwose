    3. Freshservice

    Freshservice is an American-based company founded in 2012. The site only supports the Saas platform but offers full-time support during business hours, online, and live services. The company's services include robust automation and service management on a single platform.

    The company's clientele includes Unidays and Cordant Group. The firm charges $19 per month, and customers enjoy a 21-day free trial.

    4. Semrush

    Semrush is another site similar to Brandverity. The platform has over 50 products and add-ons for online visibility management, search tools, social media, and market research. The company boasts a presence in more than 143 countries and has seven offices.

    Semrush was started in 2008 by a group of SEO three plans that are priced differently. The cheapest package costs $119 monthly, while the highest one goes for $445 per month.

    5. SpyFu

    SpyFu is an SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and PPC (Pay Per Click) tool that allows you to type a domain to see all the ranked keywords and the ads you buy on Google. The site also shows your strongest competitors. The platform is ideal for marketers or digital experts starting a campaign or who want to improve an existing one.

    8. Brandshield

    As the name suggests, Brandshield protects your brand from digital infringement and abuse. The software shields you from external threats like social media phishing, counterfeit mobile apps, and executive impersonation.

    This website was founded in 2007 and prides itself as the official measure of the digital world. Similarweb has a presence on six continents and has worked with over 3 million brands.
