    Radio star Lauren Phillips broke her arm so badly over the weekend a doctor thought she had been run over by a car. 

    The KIIS FM presenter broke four bones in her arm after suffering freak accident at friend and fellow presenter Jessica Roberts' 30th Barbie-themed birthday party on Saturday night. 

    The bash was also attended by Bec Judd and Nadia Bartel among several Melbourne socialites.  

    Speaking on KIIS 101.1’s Jase & Lauren on Monday morning, Lauren explained she took a tumble at the end of the night as she rushed to catch an Uber home. 

    'I went as Hip-Hop Barbie and the party was great. Everyone got right on into it, there were so many different Barbies,' Lauren explained. 

    Radio star Lauren Phillips broke her arm so badly over the weekend a doctor thought she had been run over by a car 

    'I was thrilled with my outfit because I wasn’t in a mini dress, I was in these cool pants so I could really hit the dancefloor.' 

    At midnight Lauren was ready to leave, and she decided to do bolt out the door without saying goodbye, because she knew she'd end up staying much longer if she did. 

    However, this decision would lead her to the emergency room. 

    'So I tried to run out the door to the Uber in my Barbie platforms and Barbie took a bit of a tumble right in front of the Uber,' she continued. 

    The KIIS FM presenter was left with four different breaks after suffering freak accident at friend and fellow presenter Jessica Roberts' 30th Barbie-themed birthday party on Saturday night 

    Once she was in the car, Lauren said she was in a lot of pain, so her fiance Paul asked if she wanted to go to the hospital, because at this point her hand was 'really blown up'. 

    However, the presenter said there was no way they were going to the emergency room dressed up as Barbie and Ken, so they decided to go home. 

    'We went home, put some ice on it,' Lauren continued. 

    'Cut to Sunday morning, I wake up with my pink eyeshadow still on and a completely blue palm of my hand.

    Pictured: The birthday girl Jessica Roberts

    'I went to the hospital, straight into X-Rays and a CT scan. Barbie is now trauma Barbie with four broken bones in her hands.'

    It was then that a doctor told Lauren that her hand was broken so badly it looked like she could've been run over by a car. 

    'I then couldn’t get it [the engagement ring] off because my fingers swelled so much,' she said. 

    'I thought they were going to cut it off, but we managed to get it off. It’s safe and well.'
