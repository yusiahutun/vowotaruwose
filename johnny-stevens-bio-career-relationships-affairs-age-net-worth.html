    Johnny Stevens Career

    Soon after his studies, Stevens began his musical career as his interest since a small age. He began jamming with his friends in the basement, leading them to perform in local bars in Cape Cod.

    Later, the band (known as Highly Suspect) shifted to Brooklyn, New York in order to build up their career, and recorded a song titled The Worst Humans. By 2015, the band completed their musical tour in the United States. Moreover, Highly Suspect is the first rock group to appear in the 300 Entertainment Roasters.
