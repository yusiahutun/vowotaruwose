    Demi Moore's Leopard String Bikini Has Us Ready for Summer

    We're not even one full day into May, but Demi Moore's vacation post has us needing summer immediately. On Tuesday (while the rest of us were stuck working inside), Moore shared a reel from her and her family's holiday in the sun.

    23 Aug 2024, 00:00

    We're not even one full day into May, but Demi Moore's vacation post has us needing summer immediately. On Tuesday (while the rest of us were stuck working inside), Moore shared a reel from her and her family's holiday in the sun.

    In the video, Moore stood inside with her three daughters Tallulah, 30, Scout, 32, Rumer Willis, their two family friends, Patrick Hilgårt and Eric Buterbaugh, Rumer's 1-year-old daughter Louetta, and of course, Moore's dog Pilaf (whom she brings everywhere). Moore, who started the clip in white baggy cargo pants and a tank top, began by running towards the camera before jumping over it.

    The video is then edited to show Moore landing on the sand having ditched her trousers for a tiny leopard-print string bikini. She posed in front of palm trees and motioned for her family to join her.

    Each daughter and friend followed suit, trading in their street clothes for swimsuits. Even Pilaf joined the group, and Rumer went back inside to grab a crawling Louetta. Rumer and her daughter Louetta twinned in matching mint-green suits, Tallulah wore a purple-and-white striped strapless bikini, and Scout sported a colorful floral two-piece.

    At the end of the video, the group struck different poses while waving and blowing kisses to the camera.

    "Family fun in the sun!" Moore captioned the cute clip. She added that the creative editing was done by Hilgårt.

    Moore and her daughters are famously close and often attend events, red carpets, and fashion shows together. Earlier this year, Moore was joined by all three of her daughters at the 2024 Vanity Fair Oscars after-party. For the star-studded soirée, Moore sported a brown satin cut-out gown with a choker details, Tallulah wore a strapless chiffon Zac Posen gown, Scout chose a silver ruffled dress (also Zac Posen), and Rumer went with a Monique Lhuillier pink strapless ballgown and matching opera gloves.
