    The Duke and Duchess of Sussex made a surprise visit to the family of a teacher killed in the Uvalde school shooting on Saturday (09.03.24).

    Prince Harry and his wife Meghan were in Uvalde, Texas, to meet with the relatives of Irma Garcia - who was one of 21 people shot dead at Robb Elementary School in May 2002 - after having been in contact with them since the tragedy.
