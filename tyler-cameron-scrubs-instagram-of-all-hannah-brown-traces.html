    Tyler Cameron Scrubs Instagram of All Hannah Brown Traces

    Hannah Brown did not find love on The Bachelorette. And yet the reality star has managed to accomplished a feat very few in her position have done before: Kept folks talking about her love life for many weeks after she stopped being The Bachelorette.

    09 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Hannah Brown did not find love on The Bachelorette.

    And yet the reality star has managed to accomplished a feat very few in her position have done before:

    Kept folks talking about her love life for many weeks after she stopped being The Bachelorette.

    That’s what happens when you select one suitor to be your fiance… dump him weeks later after you learn he had been lying about a woman back home… ask out your runner-up suitor… witness that person go on to date a supermodel… and then possibly hook up with your dancing partner.

    Let no one ever refer to Hannah Brown as  boring, that’s for sure.

    Especially when you consider rumors that she’s now back together with the guy she boinked four times in one night several months ago.

    But anyway. We digress a bit.

    The latest on Hannah and her many lovers (no judgment at all meant by that statement! Go get yours, girl!) is this:

    Tyler Cameron, who finished as the runner-up on Brown’s season of The Bachelorette and then briefly dated Hannah after she dumped Jed Wyatt, recently deleted all photos of his ex from his Instagram account.

    He scrubbed it entirely clean of the Alabama native.

    Why would he do this, considering the two follow each other and Hannah still has pictures of Tyler on her page?

    That’s a fair question, isn’t it?

    Now, a Hollywoood Life source claims to have the answer.

    "Tyler really meant no harm by removing Hannah‘s photos from his Instagram and didn’t think it was that big of a deal," this individual tells this website, adding:

    "Tyler has no hard feelings towards Hannah whatsoever, he thinks she’s a really sweet girl with a huge heart, but their relationship is in the past and he just wants to move forward from it."

    Cameron, of course created a major stir when he got romantically involved with Gigi Hadid for a little while.

    Their romance came on the heels of Tyler and Hannah going on a few dates, causing many to wonder if the well-chiseled Cameron basically ghosted Brown in favor fo Hadid.

    Brown mostly took the high road any time she was asked about Cameron, but also made it clear in multiple interview earlier this year that the stars don’t speak to each otheranymore.

    "Seeing their photos together was just a constant reminder of the past and Tyler wants to open a fresh chapter in 2020," conclude this Hollywood Life report.

    "It was nothing personal against Hannah at all, he‘s just ready for a new start in all aspects of his life…

    "He’s been meeting a ton of new people and friends and focusing his energy into that.

    "Tyler is really busy filming a new show and is making a new life for himself post Hannah and The Bachelorette franchise."
