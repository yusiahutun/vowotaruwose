    There's a new lady in Jaden Smith's life!

    The son of Will Smith and Jada Pinkett-Smith was spotted with his girlfriend Sarah Snyder at the Gypsy Sport fashion show during New York Fashion Week on Tuesday in NYC.

    The couple looked head-over-heels for each other as they cuddled during the show. They even snuck in a few sweet kisses between watching the different runway looks.

    While we don't know too much about Sarah, she was arrested for stealing a $16,000 Hermes Birkin bag in New York back in June. Yikes ... but at least she has good taste!

    It's been awhile since the 17-year-old was linked to someone. His last rumored romance was with "Keeping Up with the Kardashians" star Kylie Jenner way back in October 2013.

    While it was never confirmed, the pair spent a ton of time with one another and even attended the premiere of "Ender's Game" together.

    Clearly, both stars have since moved on -- Kylie, who's also at NYFW, is dating Tyga these days and Jaden looks pretty into Sarah.
