    Who is Dr Nowzaradan?

    Dr Nowzaradan is an American doctor, television personality, and writer. The doctor specialises in vascular and bariatric surgery. He gained popularity for appearing in My 600-lb Life, where he helps morbidly obese individuals lose weight.

    The doctor was born on October 11, 1944, in Tehran, Iran and is of Assyrian descent. He attended the university OF Tehran and graduated in 1970 with a Doctor of Medicine degree.

    Dr Nowzaradan then migrated to the United States of America, where he took part in the Medical Orientation Program at Saint Louis University in 1971. He later completed a Rotating Surgical Internship at St. John Hospital in Detroit, Michigan. On top of this, Dr Now is a fellow of the American College of Surgeons.

    The doctor is currently affiliated with Houston Obesity Surgery in Houston, Texas. He practices at various local hospitals. On top of his medical work, Dr Now is an author and has composed multiple publications on obesity and laparoscopy.
