    If you can't get enough of crossword puzzles check out OnlineCrosswords.net. It is a website that lists free printable crossword puzzles for users of all levels of expertise. You can either print crosswords or solve them online. There is also variety of crossword layouts that can be downloaded and used to create your own crosswords. The site currently lists over 8.700 free printable crossword puzzles and more added daily.

    Online Flash based interactive crossword

    If you are stuck on a word, there is a crossword help section where you can browse for words in the Puzzles Word Directory or search through the word list using the search syntax. For example, searching for B_ _K will list all the 4 letter words that start with B and end with K. Or you may try CrossTips.

    Features:

    • Resource for online and printable crossword puzzles.
    • 8.706 online crosswords in the database.
    • 7 daily printable crosswords.
    • Word directory with clues and solutions available.
    • Create your own crossword puzzles, using different layouts and sizes.

    Check out OnlineCrosswords @ www.onlinecrosswords.net
