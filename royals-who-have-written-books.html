    12 books written by the royals

    From Spare by Prince Harry to Meghan Markle's debut The Bench

      From Princess Kate's portrait book to The Duchess of Sussex's debut children's story, the royal family has offered up several good reads for book lovers to devour. But we expect none will be explosive as Prince Harry's tell-all memoir Spare, which is due to be released on 13 January.

      And these royals aren't the only ones who have put pen to paper. From King Charles to Sarah Ferguson, there have been plenty published over the years. Keep scrolling to discover 12 books written by the royals that you can still find available online…

      Prince Harry,

      by Prince Harry, The Duke of Sussex, £14, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      In Harry's candid memoir, aptly titled , he's set to reveal what life was really like growing up in the royal household. We expect he'll also offer a further insight into his life with Meghan and their two children Archie and Lilibet.

      Duchess Meghan,

      by Meghan, The Duchess of Sussex, from £8.99, Amazon

      SHOP NOW
      LISTEN ON AUDIBLE

      Inspired by Prince Harry and their son Archie, Meghan Markle's first children's book is about a father and son relationship as seen through a mother's eyes. The book is illustrated by award-winning artist Christian Robinson, and it's also available to listen to on Audible.

      Duchess Meghan,

      , £20, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      In the aftermath of the Grenfell Tower tragedy in 2017, a group of local women cooked meals for the neighbourhood. Calling themselves The Community Kitchen, Meghan went on to work closely with them and has written the foreword to their recipe book. It's packed full of simple dishes which have been passed down over generations.

      Princess Kate, 

      by The Duchess of Cambridge, £17.99, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      Princess Kate launched the Hold Still project at the beginning of the Covid-19 pandemic, inviting people of all ages from across the UK to submit a photographic portrait they had taken during lockdown. Focusing on three themes: Helpers and Heroes, Your New Normal and Acts of Kindness, the photobook includes 100 chosen submissions and will raise money for Mind and The National Portrait Gallery.

      King Charles,

      by HRH The Prince of Wales, £6.49, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      Along with environmentalist Tony Juniper and climate scientist Dr. Emily Shuckburgh, King Charles published a children's book on climate change in 2017. As an environmental leader for over forty years, he explains the challenges in a simple way that's easy for kids to understand.

      Sarah, Duchess of York,

      by Sarah Ferguson, The Duchess of York, £13.59, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      bestselling author Sarah Furgerson has made a career of writing children's books. From to , you might even remember some of them from your own childhood. This story is set on an autumn afternoon at Buttercup Cottage and is all about lending a hand.

      Sarah, Duchess of York,

      by Sarah Ferguson, The Duchess of York, from £7.99, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      Fergie's other release was in 2012. It tells the story of a feisty little girl with big dreams to become a prima ballerina and teaches children they can do anything they set their mind to. 

      Charles Spencer,

      by Charles Spencer, £22.54, Amazon

      SHOP NOW
      LISTEN ON AUDIBLE

      Princess Diana's brother, Oxford University-educated Charles Spencer, has written several books on modern history. Most recently, he published a book last year on one of England's greatest disasters, the sinking of the White Ship and the repercussions that would change European history forever.

      Pippa Middleton,

      by Pippa Middleton, £18.59, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      In 2012, Pippa released a lifestyle guide on how to celebrate throughout the year. From weekend brunches to beach picnics to Christmas parties, it includes decorating ideas, favourite recipes and lots of personal tips and tricks.

      Tom Parker Bowles,

      by Tom Parker Bowles, from £11.99, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      The Duchess of Cornwall's son Tom is a professional chef who has collaborated with Fortnum & Mason on several bestselling recipe books. is full of delicious, contemporary British dishes with expert advice on ingredients.

      Tom Parker Bowles,

      by Tom Parker Bowles, from £9.99, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      The perfect gift for any tea lover, Tom's latest book is all about how to make the most out of your cuppa, including how to make the perfect brew and a history of the hot drink. It doesn't get much more quintessentially British than this.

      Princess Marie-Chantal of Greece,

      by Princess Marie-Chantal of Greece, from £10, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      As a mother of five and founder of her successful eponymous clothing line, Marie-Chantal knows a thing or two about juggling it all. Her book offers advice on etiquette, table manners, party conversations and raising children.
