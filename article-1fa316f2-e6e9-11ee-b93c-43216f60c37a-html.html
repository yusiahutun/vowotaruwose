    County Democrats called to convene | Free News

    28 Mar 2024, 00:00

    On Saturday, in county courthouses and other public facilities across Southeast Mississippi, local Democrats will gather for their county Democratic conventions — a quadrennial ritual that traces its roots back 200 years to the nomination of Andrew Jackson for president in 1824.

    In Jones County, the meeting will be at the courthouse in Laurel. Registration opens at 9:30 a.m. and the convention will convene at 10.

    “We are calling all interested Democrats to meet us,” said Rickey Cole, chairman of the state’s 4th Congressional District Democratic executive committee. “We shall discuss issues for inclusion in our state and national party platforms, elect our county party leaders for the next four years and hear from the candidates we have nominated to serve us in public office.

    “All Democratic Party supporters are welcome and encouraged to participate.”

    For more information, call Cole at 601-342-6041, the state Democratic headquarters at 601-969-2913 or email info@mississippidemocrats.org.

    Other area Democratic Convention locations (registration at 9:30 a.m., convention at 10):

    • Wayne County, Wayne County Sheriff's Department District Courtroom, 613 Court St., Waynesboro

    • Forrest County, Paul Johnson Chancery Court Building, 641 Main St., Hattiesburg

    • George County, George County courthouse, 355 Cox St., Lucedale

    • Greene County, Greene County courthouse, 400 Main St., Leakesville

    • Pearl River County, Board of Supervisors boardroom building, Poplarville

    • Perry County, Perry County courthouse, 103 S. Main St., New Augusta

    • Stone County, Stone County courthouse, 323 Cavers St., Wiggins

    • Hancock County, Hancock County courthouse, 152 Main St., Bay St. Louis

    • Harrison County, D'Iberville Civic Center, 10395 Auto Mall Parkway, D'Iberville

    • Jackson County, Jackson County courthouse, 3104 Magnolia St., Pascagoula

    • Lamar County, Lamar County courthouse, 203 Main St., Purvis
