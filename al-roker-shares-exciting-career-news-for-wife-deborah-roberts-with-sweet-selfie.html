    05 Aug 2024, 00:00

    The Today star and his wife posed for a sweet selfie

      Al Roker shared an exciting announcement with his fans on Instagram with an adorable selfie of him and his wife Deborah Roberts.

      The Today star revealed that Deborah would be standing in as co-host on Good Morning America, joining Robin Roberts and George Stephanoupolos on the program. 

      Taking to Instagram, Al shared a snap showing him smiling next to his yawning wife, writing in the caption: "Unusual for us. @debrobertsabc heading out BEFORE me as she co-hosts @goodmorningamerica A split screen morning for us."

      Deborah, who is ABC News' Senior National Affairs Correspondent, was quick to respond to the post in the comments section, writing: "Yawning!!"

      The journalist, who is also a contributing host on alongside David Muir, co-hosted on Monday and Tuesday. She marked the exciting moment with post to Instagram.
