MartinArts to Unveil Surrealism Exhibition

MartinArts will unveil “Surreal Boundaries of Reality,” an exhibition of works by artists Christine Sobczak, LS Finch, and Maya Schonenberger, at the Court House Cultural Center gallery in Stuart March 8. The exhibit will be on view through April 20. The exhibition’s bold photomontages, paintings, and textile installations created by Sobczak, Finch, and Schonenberger lead viewers into the world of surrealism–a place where dream-like images and ideas thrive. Surrealism is not only portrayed by images of the strange and unearthly, but is also influenced and shaped by the real political, social, and environmental events around us.
