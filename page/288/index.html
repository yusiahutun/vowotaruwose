President Joe Biden Honors Elton John With National Humanities Medal During White House Concert

07 Feb 2024, 00:00

WASHINGTON, DC (CelebrityAccess) – Sir Elton John was surprised after being honored with the National Humanities Medal after giving a special performance at the White House on Friday (September 23). The President and First Lady, Dr. Jill Biden, honored John for his philanthropic contributions, including founding the Elton John AIDS Foundation in 1992. John and his husband, David Furnish, were part of the event, A Night When Hope and History Rhyme, which was organized in collaboration with The History Channel and A+E Networks and was planned to “celebrate the unifying and healing power of music, commend the life and work of Sir Elton John, and honor the every day history-makers in the audience, including teachers, nurses, frontline workers, mental health advocates, students, LGBTQ+ advocates and more,” according to an official descriptor of the event.
