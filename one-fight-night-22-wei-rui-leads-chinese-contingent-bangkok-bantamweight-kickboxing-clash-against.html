    19 May 2024, 00:00

    ONE Fight Night 22 will have a distinctly Chinese feel, with kickboxing legend Wei Rui one of three fighters from the country to have been added to the card for Bangkok on May 4.

    Wei Rui is set to take on Hiroki Akimoto in a bantamweight kickboxing bout. The Japanese fighter is a former champion and ranked No 1 in the division, meaning that the winner will be well positioned for a shot at Jonathan Haggertyâ€™s belt.

    In the flyweight division, Hu Yong (12-4) has been matched with Reece McLaren (16-9) in an MMA bout. The Chinese fighter is on a three-fight winning streak and could crack the top five for the first time with a win.

    Experienced lightweight Zhang Lipeng (34-12-2) will be fighting for the first time this year against Swiss mixed martial artist Maurice Abevi (7-1) who is a decade younger, with both men coming off first-round stoppage wins.

    The only man to ever beat Abevi is Halil Amir, who faces Akbar Abdullaev in a featherweight bout between two undefeated fighters that has all the makings of a title eliminator.

    Amir and Abdullaev both have identical 10-0 records and will both be dropping down to featherweight for this fight. Reigning champion Tang Kai will surely be watching and wondering if the winner will be his next opponent.

    As previously announced, ONE Fight Night 22 will be headlined by a strawweight Muay Thai title fight. Reigning champion Smilla Sundell faces rising star Natalia Diachkova in an all European bout.

    Meanwhile, three more fights have been added to the ONE 167 card. Liam Harrison will make his long-awaited comeback and has been matched with Katsuki Kitano in a bantamweight Muay Thai clash.

    Harrison has not fought since 2022 when he challenged Nong-O Hama for his bantamweight belt. The Englishman suffered a serious knee injury during that defeat.

    Prior to that defeat, the 38-year-old had been on a five-fight winning streak, while Kitano has won his last seven.

    The event is set for Impact Arena on June 8. Reigning ONE Championship lightweight submission grappling champion Kade Ruotolo will be making his MMA debut.

    Ruotolo, a BJJ black belt, was in action last weekend and beat Francisco Lo at ONE Fight Night 21.

    He has been matched with Blake Cooper (2-1). The Hawaiian comes from a family of famous fighters and will be looking to rebound from a stoppage loss to Abevi at ONE Fight Night 14.

    The other fight added to the card sees Mikey Musumeci against Gabriel Sousa in a bantamweight submission grappling bout. The American holds the flyweight title but is moving up a division to take on the Brazilian newcomer.

    As previously announced, this event will be headlined by an atomweight MMA fight. Stamp Fairtex is putting her title on the line against former teammate Denice Zamboanga.

    In the co-main event Tawanchai PK Saenchaimuaythaigym faces Jo Nattawut once again in an all-Thai title fight. The former is putting his featherweight Muay Thai belt on the line.
