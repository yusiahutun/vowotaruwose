    How to Choose a Travel Garment Bag?

    Here are a few aspects that you should consider before splurging on the best garment bag for travel - 

    Size: Make sure you analyze the size of the best garment bag for suits, dresses, or gowns. They are available in a variety of styles and sizes ranging from 24 to 60 inches. Please note that every garment bag is unique and equipped with plenty of mesh pockets and zippers that determine their size. Hence, do check the size and pick the best one that suits your needs. 

    Type: There is a wide range of bags like travel hanging bags, suit bags, garment duffel bags, convertible bags, tri-fold bags, etc. Each of them is distinctive in their own way. Some are minimalistic, whereas some are sturdy to help you meet your traveling needs. So, pen down your needs and then choose one of the best travel garment bags.

    Handles: Ensure that you pick one of the best travel bags for suits, gowns, and dresses, that are equipped with reinforced handles. For frequent traveling, it is advisable to invest in a wheeled garment bag that comes with push-down handles. 

    Material: Always pay heed to the material of the travel garment bag that you are vouching for. It is wise to invest in a bag that is constructed from high-grade and durable nylon and polyester fabric. Secondly, the fabric should be water-repellent and tear-resistant for added safety and security. 

    After noting down the above aspects, let us acquaint you with some tips to use the best travel garment bag accurately.

    How to Use a Travel Garment Bag?

    Following are a few tips for using a big or small garment bag appropriately -

  • Check your itinerary and pay attention to your needs. Accordingly, pick the right type of garment bag. Usually, a garment bag is used to hold formal suits, coats, or jackets.
  • Since the bag can become heavy or bulky, it is wise to store basic yet important garments and accessories like ties, belts, etc.
  • Make sure you hang your apparel on the built-in hanger first.
  • Close all the fasteners for better hold. 
  • Store your shoes in a grocery bag. 
  • Depending upon the garment bag, carefully fold it in half or in thirds. 
  • Fasten provided zippers, straps, or buckles. 
  • Pay heed to how the bag folds so that it is easier to unfold.

    FAQ's

    How do garment bags differ from suitcases?

    Garment bags are long in length and usually have a zippered closure that is ideal for carrying suits and long-length garments like gowns and dresses. On the other hand, suitcases work the best for rolled and folded clothes.

    Are garment bags made from durable materials?

    Yes, garment bags are made from nylon and polyester materials that are durable.

    What sizes are available for garment bags?

    Garment bags are available in multiple sizes ranging from 24 inches to 60 inches.

    Are travel garment bags waterproof?

    Yes, there are travel garment bags crafted from water-repellent and tear-resistant fabrics.

    Can travel garment bags be used for purposes other than travel?

    You can use travel garment bags to safely store fragile items or any dresses that are difficult to hang in cupboards.
