    Holly Madison is bringing the Prohibition era to Vegas!

    The former "Girls Next Door" star unveiled "1923 Bourbon & Burlesque" by Holly Madison at the Mandalay Bay Resort and Casino last night, a new show that aims to bring the Roaring Twenties to life on the Las Vegas Strip.

    The red-haired beauty was photographed backstage with her sexy costars, rocking a black corset with matching panties, fishnets stockings and stilettos.

    The prohibition-style bar features dancers working the room and a four-piece band nightly. Madison sings in the revue while her dancers strip down to their skivvies.

    "I'm singing, I'm doing burlesque, I'm dancing with some of the other girls, so it's a little bit of everything," Madison recently told the Las Vegas Sun. "It's not topless, but there's striptease involved. It's very sexy burlesque. It also is quite close up for that extra touch of intimacy. It's very small, and I think that that will help make it the special place it's going to be because not only does that blend itself to the speakeasy vibe, but also it's a really special feeling being there and watching the girls perform so close."

    We think it's going to be a hit!
