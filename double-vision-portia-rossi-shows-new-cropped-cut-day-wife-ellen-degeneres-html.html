    They say lovers tend to resemble one another after a while.

    And celebrity couple Portia de Rossi and Ellen DeGeneres are no exception to that rule. 

    The actress showed off a drastically shorter bob today that was just a hair longer than Ellen's trademark crop.

    Affectionate: Ellen DeGeneres and wife Portia de Rossi smile while holding hands

    The couple smiled for photographers while walking to their car in West Hollywood today.

    Dressed down for a casual day out, the usually-glamorous Portia turned heads with a clear change to her look.

    Mugging: Ellen and Portia smiled as they walked to their car - Portia having chopped off her flowing blonde locks

    Hand-in-hand: The couple married in 2008 and still look very much in love

    While her hair wasn't cut quite as short as wife Ellen, the style change made for a chic swap from her usually flowing blonde mane.

    It would seem Portia's on trend with Fifties-inspired beauty and fashion as she tucked her hair behind one ear in a look that resembled a modernised version of Mad Men's love-to-hate character Betty.

    Portia's youthful cut was hardly needed to turn back the clock for the Arrested Development star, who looks nothing like her 39 years.

    Happy together: Ellen DeGeneres and Portia de Rossi pictured together in September, 2008, long before the actress chopped off her long blonde locks

    She married Ellen, 54, in 2008.

    The couple recently purchased a home together in Beverly Hills after Ellen offloaded one of the most expensive properties - a 2.87 acre estate in the posh neighbourhood - for an astounding $49million.

    Their new 8,500-square-foot mansion is not far away, located in the exclusive Trousdale Estates. It was purchased for a more modest $18million, according to reports.
