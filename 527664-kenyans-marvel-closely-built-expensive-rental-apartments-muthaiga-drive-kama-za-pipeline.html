    Kenyans online have been comparing apartments that are closely built next to each other along the Muthaiga North Drive along the Thika Super High Way.

    The tall buildings which are adjacent to each other, despite them looking classy and very beautiful, have left many comparing the posh area to the Pipeline estate.
