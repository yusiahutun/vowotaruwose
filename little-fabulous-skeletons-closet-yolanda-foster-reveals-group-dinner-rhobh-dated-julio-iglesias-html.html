    Real Housewives' Yolanda Foster reveals that she dated Julio Iglesias

    12 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Yolanda Foster casually revealed during a group dinner on Tuesday's episode of The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills that she once dated Latin heartthrob Julio Iglesias.

    The 51-year-old reality star dropped the bombshell while dining with her fellow housewives at a swanky watefront restaurant in her native Amsterdam.

    'I've been coming here for 30 years,' Yolanda told her fellow housewives Lisa Vanderpump, Lisa Rinna, Eileen Davidson and sisters Kim and Kyle Richards. 

    Former boyfriend: Yolanda Foster revealed that she used to date Julio Iglesias during a group dinner on Tuesday night's episode of The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills

    'I used to date Julio Iglesias, don't know if you guys know him, but when I was dating him 30 years ago, he was the one who brought me here for the first time,' Yolanda said.

    Lisa Rinna immediately looked impressed upon learning that Yolanda dated the charismatic Spanish singer.

    'Wow! Yolanda is more fabulous than you realize,' she later told the camera. 

    Blast from the past: Julio, shown in the Bahamas in 1985, was an international star when he dated Yolanda

    Date night: Yolanda and Julio are shown on a dinner date in July 1986 in New York City

    'She has little fabulous skeletons in her closet that come out and say, ''Hello, I'm Julio Iglesias,''' she gushed.

    Julio, 71, was an international singing star known for songs such as To All The Girls I've Loved Before and All Of You when he and Yolanda dated.

    The Spanish native was married Filipina television host Isabel Preysler in 1971 and they had children Chabeli, Julio Jr and Enrique before their marriage was annulled in 1979. 

    She's impressed: Lisa Rinna was impressed with Yolanda's dating past

    Fabulous life: Lisa commented on how Yolanda has had a life with 'fabulous skeletons in her closet'

    Julio after his divorce lived with Dutch model Miranda Rijnsburger who is 23 years his junior and they married in August 2010.

    They have five children together including 17-year-old Miguel, 15-year-old son Rodrigo, 13-year-old twin daughters Cristina and Victoria and seven-year-old son Guillermo.

    Yolanda was married to real estate developer Mohamed Hadid from 1994 until 2000 and they had 19-year-old daughter Gigi, 18-year-old daughter Bella and 15-year-old son Anwar Hadid together. 

    First marriage: Julio is shown in 1978 with wife Isabel Preysler and their children Julio, Chabeli and Enrique

    Husband and wife: Julio is shown in 2006 with his second wife Dutch model Miranda

    The family: Miranda is shown in July 2006 with her sons Miguel and Rodrigo and twin daughters Victoria and Cristina with husband Julio

    Gigi has followed in her mother's footsteps and has become a top model.

    Yolanda in November 2011 married 65-year-old Canadian musician David Foster.

    She joined The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills in season three. 

    Happily married: Yolanda is shown with her husband David Foster in December in Goleta, California
