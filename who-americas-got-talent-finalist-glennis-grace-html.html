    Here’s what you need to know about her.

    Who is Glennis Grace?

    Glennis Grace is a Dutch musician. She was born on June 19, 1978 in Amsterdam, Netherlands.

    Her real name is Glenda Hulita Elisabeth Batta.

    She previously competed in the 50th Eurovision Song Contest, representing the Netherlands. She didn’t make it through the preliminaries.

    Glennis was first discovered when she appeared on Soundmixshow singing One Moment in Time by Whitney Houston.

    She’s part of the Dutch girl group Ladies of Soul with Candy Dulfer, Berget Lewis, Edsilia Rombley and Trijntje Oosterhuis.

    Glennis Grace net worth

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, Glennis Grace’s net worth is about $3 million (£2.2m).

    Glennis Grace YouTube

    Glennis Grace’s most recent performance on America’s Got Talent has been uploaded to the show’s YouTube page. It’s been viewed more than 800,000 times.

    America’s Got Talent finale

    The America’s Got Talent finale will be split into two parts. The first, which will be the finalist’s last performances, will take place on September 18 with the results show set for the following night.
