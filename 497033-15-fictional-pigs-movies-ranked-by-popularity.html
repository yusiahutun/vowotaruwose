    Pigs have a bad reputation in the real world for rolling in the mud and being filthy. However, the story is different in the fictional realm. The four-legged creatures are recognized for their striking looks, delightful charm, and keen intellect. This explains why pigs have popped up in box office hits in the last few decades. But which are the most famous fictional pigs in movies.

    Since the beginning of cinema, animals have played a part in films. It doesn't matter if they are cast as a side character or the main protagonist. They have an indelible place in the movies thanks to their personality or appearance or an unforgettable mixture of both. Fictional pigs are some of the most beloved animal characters.

    List of fictional pigs in movies

    When it comes to fictional pig characters, there are a few that stand out from the rest. Besides Miss Piggy and the famous Three Little Pigs, there are many other fictional pig characters in movies. Here are the most famous pigs on TV.

    1. Peppa Pig - Peppa Pig

    Peppa is one of the most famous pigs in cartoons. She is the main character in Peppa Pig. The outgoing preschool pig lives with her mummy, daddy, and her little brother, George. Peppa Pig participates in many energetic activities.

    She learns something new every day and has a lot of fun with her family and friends. Her adventures are fun, sometimes involve a few tears, but always end happily with loud snorts of laughter.

    2. Porky Pig - Looney Tunes

    Porky Pig is an animated character featuring in the Warner Bros. Looney Tunes series of cartoons. It was the first true star character to be adorned in the franchise. The character was originally created by Friz Feleng before it was revamped and turned into a cartoon character by Chuck Jones.

    Porky Pig is renowned for his signature line at the end of each short, "Th-Th-Th-That's All Folks!" Bob Bergen voices the character of Porky Pig in Looney Tunes.

    3. Miss Piggy - The Muppets

    Are you looking for a Disney movie with a pig in it? Then The Muppets should be at the top of your list. Miss Piggy is one of the characters featured in the series. She is renowned for her breakout role in The Muppet Show.

    Miss Piggy began as a relatively minor character. Over the years, she has risen through the ranks to attain the leading-lady status on the show. Miss Piggy tends to use French phrases in her speech and is a karate expert.

    4. Fifer Pig, Fiddler Pig, and Practical Pig - Three Little Pigs

    The Three Little Pigs is a famous pig movie. The Silly Symphony cartoon was produced by Walt Disney in 1933. The production cast includes three pig characters Fifer Pig, Fiddler Pig, and Practical Pig. Fifer and Fiddler are named for their musical instruments of choice.

    On the other hand, Practical pig is named for his logical and no-nonsense demeanor. These pig characters are the constant targets of the big bad wolf. They are renowned for building houses from straw, sticks, and bricks.

    5. Wilbur - Charlotte's Web

    Wilbur is the main protagonist of the film Charlotte's Web. The pig resides on Mr. Zuckerman's farm. Wilbur is lighthearted and playful. He desires friends and frequently pursues friendships with those around him.

    He has a devoted friend Charlotte, the spider who saves his life. Charlotte writes about him in her web, and people from all over visit the farm to see this special pig

    6. Hamm - Toy Story

    Hamm is a supporting character in the Toy Story series. He is a wisecracking realist plastic piggy bank with a cork in his belly. The pig originally belonged to Andy Davis but now belongs to Bonnie Anderson. Hamm appears to be best friends with Mr. Potato Head. Hamm is a technological genius. He can identify types of trash bags and child locks.

    7. Hamton J. Pig - Tiny Toon Adventures

    Hamton is one of the most famous pigs. The famous cartoon character is from the Warner Bros. animated series Tiny Toon Adventures. His character is voiced primarily by Don Messick. In the movie, he is depicted as a young male pig who wears blue overalls. Hamilton is one of the animals at the Acme farm. He is one of the animals that Elmyra Duff is not interested in.

    8. Piglet - Winnie-the-Pooh

    Piglet is a fictional character from Walt Disney's Winnie The Pooh movie. He is Winnie‑the‑Pooh's closest friend among all the toys and animals featured in the series. Piglet is depicted as a very small animal with a timid disposition. He is ashamed of being small and clumsy and wanders off into the Hundred Acre Wood, prompting his friends to organize a search party to find him.

    9. King Pig - Angry Birds

    King Pig is the main antagonist of the Angry Birds series. He is the former monarch of the Piggy kingdom. King Pig is depicted as an anthropomorphic pig with green skin. As the diabolical king of the pigs, King Pig embodies all of the worst traits of Pig-kind. He's tyrannical, greedy, and selfish. His loyal subjects are forced to serve him constantly.

    10. Hen Wen - The Black Cauldron

    Hen Wen is the magical pet pig in The Black Cauldron. The magical oracular pig can tell the future. This is the reason why she was kidnapped by an evil lord known as the Horned King. Later, the king realizes that the pig is useless to him and orders Taran to behead her.

    11. McDull - My Life as McDull

    McDull is a famous cartoon pig character created by Alice Mak and Brian Tse. This cartoon character has appeared in several comics since the 1990s. The male pig can be distinguished by a birthmark on his right eye. He has a heart of gold, but he isn't very smart. His attitude towards life makes him a popular character.

    12. Babe - Babe: Pig in the City

    Babe is the main antagonist in Babe: Pig in the City. Babe returns to Farmer Hoggett's farm after his victory in the sheepherding contest. Unfortunately, the farmer is injured and unable to work. Babe is the only one who can save the farm. He crosses the ocean and moves to the big city to demonstrate his talents as a sheep herder.

    13. Porco Rosso - Porco Rosso

    This is a 1992 Japanese animated adventure-fantasy film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki. Porco Rosso is the main antagonist in the film. The plot revolves around an Italian World War I ex-fighter ace, Marco Pagot, who is transformed into an anthropomorphic pig by a curse.

    14. Olivia - Olivia

    This is a children's computer-animated comedy television series produced by media company Chorion. Olivia is the main character in the series. She is depicted as an imaginative and creative pig. Olivia does everything with her best friends, Julian and Francine.

    15. Petunia Pig - Looney Tunes

    The list of famous pigs cannot be complete without the mention of Petunia Pig. She is an animated cartoon character in Warner Bros Looney Tunes. She looks much like Porky Pig, except that she wears a dress and has pigtailed black hair. Her character is voiced most times by Grey DeLisle-Griffin.
