    Early life

    The singer was born Nona Carol Dirksmeyer on 26 December 1985 in Zachary, East Baton Rouge Parish, Louisiana, United States. She was the daughter of Paul Dean Dirksmeyer (father) and Carol Yvette Larpenter Dirksmeyer (mother).

    Unfortunately, Nona Dirksmeyer's father died when she was only 11 years old in 1996. On the other hand, her mother remarried when Nona joined college. Nona decided to get her apartment as she did not like some of her stepfather's strict rules.

    The late student grew up in a close-knit community in Louisiana, surrounded by the warmth of her family. Dreams of music and beauty marked her childhood and her desire to make a difference.

    She attended Dover High School, where her talent blossomed. After graduating high school in 2004, she began her academic journey at Arkansas Tech University. Nona was a sophomore majoring in music education and actively involved in the Concert Chorale and the Chamber Choir.

    What happened to the beauty queen Nona?

    On 14 December 2005, Kevin Jones met with Nona and drove home to Dover after midnight. He knew Nona had plans for the next day, 15 December, because she had told him about the Big Sisters' organisation.

    Kevin also had plans because he told his mother, Janice, the school librarian, that he would take her to the teachers’ Christmas party. But as they went to the party, Kevin told Janice he was worried about Nona as she had not responded to his calls that afternoon.

    He asked a friend, Ryan, to check on Nona as he worked close to her apartment. Ryan told him that her upstairs light was on, but there was no answer when he knocked on the door. Kevin went to Nona's apartment with his mother and linked with Ryan.

    They knocked on Nona's door multiple times, but there was no answer. Although Kevin had a key to Nona's apartment, he did not have it on him, so he went around to the back of the apartment and found the sliding door open. According to Daily Mail Online, the late Nona was lying on the floor in a pool of blood inside the apartment.

    The investigations commenced, and the police arrested Kevin Jones and questioned him. However, they did not charge him until 2006, when they tried him in court for Nona’s murder.

    Kevin’s case continued for a long time, with the prosecutor and defence presenting to the judge. But the jury found Kevin Jones not guilty of first-degree murder. It also emerged that the police suspected another man, Nona’s neighbour, Gary Dunn. So, the police arrested Gary for murder.

    During Gary’s trial, the court heard that the contraceptive wrapper was tested for DNA, and it matched Gary's DNA. But after a long case, Gary’s trial ended with a deadlocked Jury. Because of Gary Dunn's mistrial, a second trial was announced but had the same results.

    The prosecutors could not try him a third time and asked the judge to dismiss the charges against him. Gary was a free man.

    According to USA Today, Gary Dunn is in prison for attempting to kidnap at least three women, including Miss Arkansas USA 2022, Rylie Wagner. He pleaded guilty during trial and accepted a plea deal for attempted kidnapping and indecent exposure charges.

    Dunn is being held at the Arkansas Department of Correction, Wrightsville Unit and sentenced to 15 years. Also, Gary was incarcerated at the corrections facility at least four times, most recently in November 2019. His previous offences include possession of firearms and second-degree battery.
