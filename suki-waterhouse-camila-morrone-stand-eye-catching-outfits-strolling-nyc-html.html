    Suki Waterhouse and Camila Morrone stand out in eye-catching outfits while strolling in NYC

    Suki Waterhouse and Camila Morrone braved the cold in style as they stepped out in Midtown Manhattan on Wednesday afternoon. The 31-year-old singer and the 25-year-old actress both cut fashionable figures as they each took a stroll along the Big Apple's sidewalks.

    16 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Suki Waterhouse and Camila Morrone braved the cold in style as they stepped out in Midtown Manhattan on Wednesday afternoon.

    The 31-year-old singer and the 25-year-old actress both cut fashionable figures as they each took a stroll along the Big Apple's sidewalks.

    The pair are both set to star in the upcoming miniseries Daisy Jones & The Six, and the latter of the two recently promoted the show during an appearance on Today. 

    Waterhouse looked lovely in a vibrantly patterned yellow-and-brown dress that featured a jet-black collar during her outing.

    The Assassination Nation star stayed comfortable in the chilly winter weather while wearing a sizable fur coat.

    Out and about: Suki Waterhouse and Camila Morrone were spotted while stepping out in Midtown Manhattan on Wednesday afternoon

    The songwriter also donned a pair of knee-high brown leather boots and wore a stylish pair of sunglasses. 

    Her gorgeous blonde locks cascaded onto her shoulders for the length of her stroll.

    Morrone opted for an all-white outfit that included a cropped jacket that showcased her sculpted midsection.

    The Death Wish actress also wore a pair of wide-legged pants and high-heeled shoes.

    The former girlfriend of Leonardo DiCaprio accessorized with several sparkling earrings.

    Her voluminous brunette locks remained free-flowing and contrasted perfectly with the light tone of her clothing.

    Development on Daisy Jones & The Six was revealed to the public in 2018, when producer Reese Witherspoon was first attached to the project.

    The show is based on Taylor Jenkins Reid's novel of the same name, which was published in 2019. 

    Making an impression: Waterhouse looked lovely in a vibrantly patterned yellow-and-brown dress that featured a jet-black collar during her outing

    Finishing it off: The Death Wish actress also wore a pair of wide-legged pants and high-heeled shoes

    The program is set in the 1970s and is centered on the rise and fall of a Los Angeles-based rock band. 

    It was revealed that Morrone had signed on to appear in the show that year, with Waterhouse being added to the cast in February of 2020.

    Other cast members in the project include Riley Keough, Sam Claflin and Josh Whitehouse.

    Physical production on the project took place from September of 2021 until May of last year.

    Waterhouse and Morrone recently spoke to Town & Country, and the former revealed that she had grown especially close to her costar.

    Ready to go: It was revealed that Morrone had signed on to appear in the show that year, with Waterhouse being added to the cast in February of 2020

    'When you meet someone and start working with them and spend like 12 hours a day together for two years, you know everything about each other's lives and start dissecting everything and that bleeds into the show,' she recalled.

    The singer went on to express that she and the actress had made the most of their time in character during the show's production.

    'It's always very fun, even when we're sitting in the background and there's something else going on, and Karen and Camila get to gossip, and we know that it's not going to be in the show,' she said.

    Morrone also remarked that she was previously unaware of the fandom that had grown around the project as it was being made. 

    'Seeing the world excited for this show has made me happy that I didn't know how important it was gonna be at the time,' she said.

    Daisy Jones & The Six's first three episodes are set to be made available on the Amazon Prime Video streaming service this Friday.
