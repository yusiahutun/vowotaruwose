    Fred Arocho Quits Citizen TV Role Over Conflict of Interest with TV 47

    Renowned sports journalist Fred Arocho has bid farewell to his role at Citizen TV after an impressive four-year stint. In an emotional statement released on Wednesday, March 6, Arocho expressed his gratitude to the Royal Media Services (RMS) management for the opportunity to serve in the esteemed media house.

    08 Feb 2024, 00:00
    • Fred Arocho surprised fans with his departure from Citizen TV's Sporty Monday Show after four years
    • Speculation has arisen regarding the reason for the journalist's exit, but a close source revealed the reason behind Arocho's story
    • According to the source, there was a conflict of interest due to his move to TV 47 and the station also increased his salary to sweeten the deal

    Renowned sports journalist Fred Arocho has bid farewell to his role at Citizen TV after an impressive four-year stint.

    In an emotional statement released on Wednesday, March 6, Arocho expressed his gratitude to the Royal Media Services (RMS) management for the opportunity to serve in the esteemed media house.

    Fred Arocho bids Citizen TV farewell

    During his tenure, Arocho was a key contributor to Citizen TV’s Sporty Monday Show.

    He reminisced about the past four years, describing them as extraordinary moments.

    In his heartfelt message, Arocho extended special regards to several individuals who made his journey memorable:

    “It’s been 4 yrs of great moments at Citizen TV’s Sporty Monday Show. As I pen off, may I take this opportunity to thank the management of Royal Media Service for the opportunity to serve, fellow panellists Mukami Wambora, Steve Shitera, and Zitah Jimoi, special regards to The Head of RMS Sports Centre Isaac Swila for the memorable moments at work. I have truly enjoyed every bit of my stay,” Arocho stated.

    Why Fred Arocho left Citizen TV

    Behind the scenes, a significant development led to Arocho's departure.

    His recent move to TV 47 triggered a chain of events.

    Previously associated with Radio Jambo, Arocho transitioned to Radio 47 in 2023. However, this shift had implications for his role at Citizen TV.

    According to a source, Arocho was informed that continuing as a panellist on Citizen TV while being associated with TV 47—a direct competitor—posed potential conflicts.

    The source revealed that the media house increased Arocho's salary to allow him to concentrate on his new responsibilities fully.

    "Arocho was told by TV 47 that Citizen TV is a competitor, and there was a conflict of interest. So he had to quit the job. His salary was raised to enable him to concentrate on his job at TV 47," the source told TUKO.co.ke.

    Why did Fred Aroch leave Radio Jambo?

    Previously, Arocho opened up about the reasons that led him to make the radio switch.

    Arocho had a stellar career on Radio Jambo where he wowed fans with his mastery in announcing football matches to listeners.

    He added that greener pastures and facing a new challenge made him ditch Radio Jambo for Radio 47.
