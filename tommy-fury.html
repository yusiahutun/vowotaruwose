    Tommy is currently playing in the light-heavyweight division, has a professional boxing record of 8 fights, and has won all 9 of them.

    Tommy Fury, 24, was barred from traveling to the United States in August 2022 for a boxing match with YouTube sensation Jake Paul.

    Jake Paul had been challenging Tommy constantly for the fight, which seemed to be the most awaited match among fans.

    After two unsuccessful attempts to fight, Paul and Jake agreed to terms on a boxing match in Saudi Arabia.

    Fury won the fight via split decision in a close bout on 27 February 2023 in Diriyah.

    The news about their rematch is swirling in the media currently, but it seems like we have to wait much longer for that to happen.

    Moreover, Fury seems very experimental because he took a break from his boxing career to participate in the reality show ‘Love Island 2019.’

    Early Life & Family, Education

    Tommy Fury was born in Manchester, England, to parents John Fury Sr and Chantal Fury. He comes from a family that has a history in the field of boxing. 

    Starting from his Father, John Fury Sr., also called ‘Gipsy,’ was a successful pro boxer during the ’80s & ’90s. 

    Similarly, talking about the Fury family history in boxing, we can’t leave behind Tyson Fury, also known as ‘Gipsy King.’ Tyson Fury is a half-brother of Tommy.

    Likewise, Fury’s cousin Hughie Fury is a Professional British Boxer who won a gold medal in the super-heavyweight division during the Youth World Championships.

    Moreover, Tommy’s Father, who is Irish-English-born, married Amber in 1964. 

    As for his education, Tommy attended St. Ambrose Barlow High School in Manchester, United Kingdom.

    Sadly, there is no information on current enrollment for Tommy in any higher education.

    After completing high school, he took a break to participate in the Love Island TV reality show.

    How old is Tommy Fury? Age, Height & Body Measurement

    Standing 1.8 m tall and weighing 165 lbs, Fury has very appealing charisma. He has a muscular built-up physique with a broad shoulder & a V-line in the waist.

    He says boxing and gym are his secrets of maintenance. Moreover, Tommy sticks to a strict fitness routine and diet plan.

    Not to mention Fury was also very popular among the female audience during the show due to his looks and body type. He is a very beautiful, talented boxer.

    Career

    Tommy started his boxing career in his childhood. Being from a boxer’s family also added to his passion for boxing since his early childhood.

    Currently, Tommy plays in the category of all light heavyweight boxers weighing above 165 to 178 pounds.

    Before transitioning into the light-heavyweight category, Fury had 12 amateur fights.

    After that, Tommy, also known by the nickname TNT, made a contract by signing a promotional deal with Fran Warren.

    He made his debut against Jevgenijs Andrejevs, twice his age, on December 22, 2018, at the Manchester Arena.

    Tommy was a much-looked boxer compared to his brother Tyson, a renowned boxer.

    Moreover, Fury proved every doubter wrong and was here to stay. He established himself as a professional boxer with three back-to-back victories from his opening matches.

    Likewise, three months after his debut, Fury was set up in the boxing arena with fellow British boxer Callum Ide at Leicester Arena.

    Tommy performed exceptionally by knocking him out in the first round, where Callum received his 28th defeat in 30 fights. 

    Similarly, it only took Fury 62 seconds to knock down Przemyslaw Binienda of Poland at Copper Box Arena, Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park.

    This victory garnered huge attention towards Fury, and he made his mark on his boxing career.

    Reality Show Career

    In 2019, Tommy took a break from boxing after two bouts and joined season 5 of the TV reality show Love Island.

    The show is about a group of “Islander” who gets a chance to stay in the stunning villa with the hope of finding their soul mate while enjoying the summer.

    Tommy started in the villa by connecting by dating Lucie on day two, but they couldn’t make it a long way.

    After that, he got linked up with Molly Mae Hague within just five days of entering the villa. The two had very appealing chemistry on screen and finished as runners-up.

    Who is Tommy Fury Dating? Personal Life, Girlfriend

    Currently, Fury is in a relationship with Molly Mae Hague, whom he met on the TV reality show Love Island.

    Molly is a British model and social media influencer with over 7.4 million Instagram followers. She was born on May 25, 1999, in England.

    Molly and Tommy had terrific chemistry in the show; everyone thought it was only limited up to the end of the show.

    However, their relationship did not just end at the show; they have become inseparable since the show’s end.

    The couple bought their apartment together and shared the news of Molly’s pregnancy. 

    They welcomed their daughter Bambi on 23rd January 2023.

    Before being in a relationship with Molly, Tommy was in a relationship with Millie Roberts, but they broke up in March 2019.

    After two unsuccessful attempts to fight, Paul and Jake agreed to terms on a boxing match in Saudi Arabia.

    Fury won the fight via split decision in a close bout on 27 February 2023 in Diriyah.

    Tommy not only won the fight but also ended Paul’s unbeaten streak in boxing.

    The highly anticipated fight was attended by former heavyweight champion boxer Mike Tyson, soccer player Cristiano Ronaldo, comedian Kevin Hart, and his older brother Tyson Fury.
