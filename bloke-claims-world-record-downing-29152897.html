    Bloke claims world record for downing Jaffa Cakes in three minutes could you beat him?

    07 Jul 2024, 00:00

    A bloke reckons he’s smashed a world record by devouring an incredible 102 Jaffa Cakes in a gut-busting eating competition.

    With a pint of warm water in hand Max Stanford, 35, shoved two of the tasty treats down his gob at a time, chomping past his own record of 88.

    Also known as Max vs Food, he now claims that he’s set a new world record following his efforts last week.

    Meanwhile, in fourth, was Tunnysvsfood who still managed to eat a respectable 50 of the chocolate orange sweets.

    Max’s total intake saw him ingest a whopping 4,692 calories in the process, making his recommended calorie intake for the day of 2.500 just a dot in his rearview mirror.

    The competition at The George Pub and Grill in Stockton-on-Tees, County Durham isn’t his first victory though.

    Max, from Brixton, southwest London, has also managed to put away a very impressive whole kilogram of popular children's cereal Frosties in six minutes.

    Max, said: "It went very, very well, I surprised myself. I beat my record from last year of 88.

    "It wasn't too bad eating that many. My tactic was eating and then using a lot of water and getting into a rhythm.
