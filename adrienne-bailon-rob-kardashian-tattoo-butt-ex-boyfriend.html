    Adrienne Bailon’s Rob Kardashian Tattoo Removed From Butt: Erasing Him For Good

    Adrienne now dates Roc Nation’s Lenny S — and she tells Extra all about her journey to remove her Rob tattoo for good. 

    “Not my favorite tattoo,” she admits. “I’ve waited for a really long time — long enough — to have this officially gone. I’ve actually had two sessions before this and I’d come home and see no difference and it looks like I just got a fresh tattoo.”

    Adrienne’s the first to admit she’s beyond ready for Rob’s name to be gone.

    “Living the last six years with somebody’s name on your body is a little weird,” she spills to Extra.

    Adrienne went to Dr. Jeremy Brauer of The Laser and Skin Surgery Center in New York City for her tattoo removal. He used the PicoSure laser to get rid of “Rob.”

    Watch the video of her tattoo removal process below!

    Adrienne Bailon: How Her Boyfriend Feels About Her Rob Kardashian Tattoo

    Adrienne says her boyfriend has been really supportive about the awkward tat.

    “It’s great that he’s somebody that wants it gone, but not in a selfish way,” she reveals.

    “He wants it to be done right, and it won’t be something that will damage my skin and I’ll be scarred for life,” she says during the interview.
