    Who is Maya Hawke?

    Maya Hawke is an American actress and model. Maya was born in New York City and was charmed by acting.

    Like both her mother and grandmother, Hawke modeled for Vogue at the start of her career.

    She was also chosen as the face of the British fashion retailer All Saints’ 2016/2017 collection. Her recent project includes the film Once Upon a Time in Hollywood.
