    Miri Marshall is a Meteorologist at WUSA9; Learn her Salary and Net Worth

    Miri Marshall joined the WUSA9's weather team in September 2017. She forecasts the weather on Wake Up Washington, each weekday morning from 4:30 am to 7 am. 

    Before WUSA, the LSU alum covered weather for WBAL-TV in Baltimore, KCEN in Waco, TX, and KFOX TV in El Paso, TX. However, her first job on television was not weather-related. She started her career as a desk assistant, assignment editor, and field producer at ABC News in Los Angeles.
