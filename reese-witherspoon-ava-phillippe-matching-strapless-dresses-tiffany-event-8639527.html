    Ava Phillippe is already a carbon copy of her mom Reese Witherspoon, and on Thursday, the actress and her doppelgänger daughter took their resemblance to a whole new level in matching outfits.

    Witherspoon and Phillippe attended the launch of Tiffany & Co.'s Blue Book 2024: Tiffany Céleste at The Beverly Estate in Beverly Hills in coordinating strapless gowns that had the two looking identical. Witherspoon wore an ankle-length little black dress with a thigh-high leg slit, bedazzled hoop detailing on her hip, and a silk ribbon woven through it. Of course, she tied it with a bow.

    Her chunky sparkly necklace complemented the frock, and she added diamond stud earrings, a matching bangle, and blingy rings. Witherspoon finished off the look with black strappy heels, and she styled her signature blonde hair in glamorous waves with a deep part and side bangs swept across her forehead. A bronze contoured complexion, feathered lashes, and a peach lip completed her glam.

    For her part, Phillippe also wore a strapless gown. However, the Alberta Ferretti number was a deep charcoal gray color and featured a structured bodice with midsection boning. Her accessories included a statement-making gold diamond choker, as well as coordinating earrings and rings. Her blonde hair was pulled into a bun, save for a few curly face-framing tendrils. She kept her glam fresh with a dewy complexion, voluminous lashes, and a subtle pink lip.

    The mother-daughter duo are no strangers to slaying together. As of late, they've made a habit out of attending fabulous fashion events together in their best dress. Case in point? Earlier this year they stepped out together for the Fendi Haute Couture spring/summer 2024 show in Paris looking effortlessly cool and chic.

    The two skipped the matching moment that time and chose two very different (but still complementary) OOTDs. Witherspoon wore another LBD while Phillippe opted for a beige leather shorts, a gray trench coat, and a yellow turtleneck sweater.
