    Succession Season 4 Episode 10: The Epic Finale and Kendall Roy's Trajectory

    We know that Succession season 4 episode 10 is going to be the series finale and, of course, it is going to be emotional. How can it not? It also seems as though there is a certain trajectory that Kendall Roy is on at this point that cannot be ignored. From what we know about the show at the moment, here’s what we can say — Kendall may end up in charge at the end of all of this, but he may also end up alone. He’s losing a lot of people close to him, and what little morality he had is falling by the wayside.

    Jeremy Strong's Insights into Kendall's Journey

    So what does Jeremy Strong have to say at present about the story to come? Speaking on the show’s official podcast via The Hollywood Reporter, here is what the actor had to say:

    “Starting from the end of episode six, really, I think he can see the endgame. A lot of things happen in the ninth episode. Him feeling blamed for the election and his culpability in making this kind of Faustian bargain, really, he’s compromised himself utterly, and he knows it. I think he’s in turmoil. The real moral jeopardy that we see in the previous episode."

    Jeremy Strong's words provide a glimpse into Kendall's state of mind and the challenges he faces. Let's explore the possible outcomes and developments in the series finale.

    → On next - Succession Season 4 Episode 8 spoiler

    Kendall's Losses and Struggles

    1. Losing Close Relationships

    Kendall has been experiencing significant losses throughout the series, especially when it comes to people close to him. His ex-wife Rava calling him out on his actions and taking their children out of the city was a hard blow. Additionally, his assistant Jess, who was one of the few people Kendall trusted, also defects. Losing these connections has left Kendall feeling vulnerable and alone. The absence of support intensifies his determination to pursue his ambitions.

    2. The Unsettling Effect

    Kendall's world has been upended by the recent events, leaving him unseated and in turmoil. The betrayal from those he considered loyal has caused a deep wound. The void left by their departure sharpens his desire to achieve his goals, pushing him to double down on his efforts. Kendall's unrelenting pursuit becomes a means to reclaim control and prove himself.

    3. Moral Jeopardy and Faustian Bargains

    Throughout the series, Kendall has faced moral dilemmas and made questionable choices. He has compromised his values and engaged in Faustian bargains to further his ambitions. The weight of his actions and their consequences weigh heavily on him. Kendall finds himself at a moral crossroads, uncertain about the path he should take.

    Kendall's Dark Side and Lingering Skeletons

    4. The Killer Within

    Will Kendall embrace his dark side and become the "killer" that his father once claimed he couldn't be? This question lingers as the series finale approaches. Kendall's journey has been a constant struggle between his desire for power and the remnants of his conscience. Whether he fully possesses the skills and mindset to become the ruthless force his father expected remains unclear.

    5. Skeletons from the Past

    As Kendall's story unfolds, there are concerns that the events of the past, such as the shocking revelation at the end of season 1, may resurface. The skeletons buried in Kendall's closet could potentially come back to haunt him and disrupt his trajectory. The lingering secrets add an extra layer of suspense and uncertainty to the narrative.
