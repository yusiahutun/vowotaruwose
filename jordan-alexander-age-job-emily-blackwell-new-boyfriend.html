    Made in Chelsea: Bali all the way from Indonesia kicked off on E4 on 20 December 2022. And for its cast, it brought back several OG stars, including Emily Blackwell (and some unfamiliar faces).

    So, after her stormy relationship with Harvey Armstrong played out in the series’ previous season, Emily returned to the screen again, and with a new beau called Jordan Alexander. So, in the rest of the writing, we shall discuss who Emily Blackwell’s new boyfriend is.

    Made In Chelsea: Meet Jordan Alexander, Emily Blackwell’s New Boyfriend

    Emily Blackwell went Instagram official with Jordan Alexander after he was teased in the trailer for the new Made in Chelsea: Bali. Yes, Jordan appears on this new project of E4 as Emily’s boyfriend.

    Anyway, after showing off these many photos from what appeared to be a romantic getaway, the new couple also received the seal of approval not only from Emily’s best pal Miles Nazaire, who revealed his romantic feelings for her during the last season of the show but also from her co-stars Tiffany Watson and Sophie Habboo.

    Ever since this new man of Emily has been seen on her social media several times. They celebrated Christmas together more recently and before that in November, Jordan wished her a ‘happy birthday” all the way from Bali.

    Before this, Emily dumped her ex Harvey after he cheated on her. Harvey apparently had bedded another woman behind Emily’s back and lied about it before he was caught out with an incriminating shot. Emily and he had been together for two years already when it happened.

    All of this is in the past now as Emily has moved on and she also spoke to OK! Magazine (in October 2022) about not having hard feelings towards her ex. In fact, looking at the bigger picture, she thanked him for cheating on her. Because, she said, she is “so much more happier now with the right person.”

    Harvey, meanwhile, broke down and vowed to win back his Emily in an episode of Made In Chelsea that aired in April 2022.

    Jordan Alexander Age

    Jordan Alexander was born in 1990. So, he turned 32 years old in 2022.

    Jordan Alexander Job

    Since January 2019, Jordan Alexander has been working full-time as a managing director at AEJ Consulting Ltd. AEJ Consulting happens to be a London and Reading-based recruitment consultancy, dedicated to sourcing and delivering talent within Technology & Transformation, Sales, Consulting, and Finance.

    Jordan, on his LinkedIn, writes how he feels takes pride in a) leading a professional and highly motivated recruitment business b) being responsible for defining the vision of AEJ Consulting c) executing the overall strategic vision, and leading their agile and efficient workforce, and so on.

    He then goes on to cite that he received recommendations from Greg Newman, the CEO of Onyx Capital Group – The World’s Largest Liquidity Provider in Oil Swaps, and a few others. Greg was Jordan’s client.

    Long before taking on this journey in recruitment consultancy, Jordan studied at Epsom College (between 2003 and 2008).

    Is Jordan Alexander On Instagram?

    Yes. Jordan Alexander could be found on Instagram @jordan_alexander24, with 105 posts and 7,668 followers, as of 25 December 2022.

    Besides, Jordan also regularly appeared on his Instagram popular beau’s personal blog @emily.blackwell_ where she entertained a huge 340K followers.

Also, she originally comes from Newport, Isle of Wight celebrates her birthday on September 28th.
