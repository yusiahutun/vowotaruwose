    Barstool Sports CEO Erika Nardini has left her banker husband Brett for her married squash coach, according to reports. 

    The businesswoman is said to have met pro player Yvain 'Swiss' Badanat at her Connecticut members club last summer, according to Page Six.  

    She was first seen seen kissing a man that was not her husband back in April, the paper reports. A source at the time said she 'seemed real excited'. 

    The alleged affair marks the latest scandal to hit popular blog Barstool after a sex tape featuring its founder Dave Portnoy leaked earlier this month. 

    In that video, a naked Portnoy, 44, is seen sitting atop aspiring model Sydney Raines as she wears a black leather dog collar around her neck. The Bartstool Sports star is then seen yanking the collar with a metal chain. 

    Erika is said to have first met Yvain at New Canaan Country Club in Connecticut last summer. His wife Cynthia reportedly grew suspicious when the world-ranked squash professional began arriving home in the early hours.

    After reportedly finding messages between the pair in which they discussed having children and getting a dog Cynthia filed for divorce in March.  

    Erika and Brett - who have two young sons - have also split, Page Six reports.

    Cynthia and Yvain were married in 2012 and have four sons aged between 17 and 26.

    They have been described as 'the local golden couple' and were said to have been planning their retirement before the split. A source said: 'They were madly in love.'

    In January of 2020, the company was valued at $450 million, with Penn National Gaming buying a 36 per cent stake for $163 million.
