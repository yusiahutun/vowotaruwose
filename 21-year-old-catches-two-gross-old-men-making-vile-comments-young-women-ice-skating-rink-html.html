    A 21-year-old ballerina caught two 'gross old men' making vile comments about her and other young women at an ice skating rink - despite both guys admitting that they 'shouldn't be looking' because the skaters were 'too young.'

    11 Jun 2024, 00:00

    A 21-year-old ballerina caught two 'gross old men' making vile comments about her and other young women at an ice skating rink - despite both guys admitting that they 'shouldn't be looking' because the skaters were 'too young.'

    Luna Montana, from California, wanted to try figure skating so she signed herself up for some lessons at a local rink.

    She video-taped herself throughout her first lesson to use for a YouTube video - but was shocked when she watched the footage back and discovered that she had accidentally caught a 'disturbing' conversation between two men who were sitting in the stands and watching her and the other young skaters practice their moves. 

    The men, who did not seem to be aware their conversation was being recorded, made many 'disgusting' comments about how they couldn't stop watching the women skate - and one, who said he was 52 years old, even claimed he was 'dreaming' about them, despite admitting that they're most likely underage. 

    Luna uploaded the uncomfortable moment to her TikTok account where it quickly went viral, gaining more than five million views in a matter of days - and leaving many people sickened by their words.
