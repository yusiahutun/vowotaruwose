    SkinCeuticals Phyto A+ Brightening Treatment

    Best for sensitive skin

    SkinCeuticals Phyto A+ Brightening Treatment

    Skinceuticals' gel-like moisturiser is one worth paying attention to if you struggle to bring down redness and breakouts in the warmer months. It's extremely light, yet provides enough moisture to make dry, tight skin feel soothed and hydrated. With a modest 3 per cent azelaic acid and 2 per cent alpha arbutin (a brightening antioxidant that tackles melasma and sun spots) it's a gentle yet powerful option for easily upset skin.

    Best for rosacea

    Dr. Sam's Flawless Brightly Serum

    Best for rosacea

    Dr. Sam's Flawless Brightly Serum

    Credit: Dr. Sam's

    Dr. Bunting's own azelaic acid serum is a true favourite among the Bazaar beauty team – so much so, we gave it a Best of the Best award in the year that it launched.

    Here, 10 per cent azelaic acid is combined with pore-clearing niacinamide and a gentle (read: acne-friendly) antioxidant to create a fast-absorbing serum that treats inflammatory flares, breakouts and uneven skin tone. Start with half a pump, every other day.

    Best for brightening

    Drunk Elephant Bouncy Brightfacial™

    Best for brightening

    Drunk Elephant Bouncy Brightfacial™

    Credit: Space NK

    Drunk Elephant's latest launch has really impressed us in its ability to fade post-acne scarring. With a significant dose of azelaic acid cocktailed with salicylic acid, it'll help keep breakout-prone skin clear while fading all forms of pigmentation too. Apply it to cleansed skin in the evening – if you're very sensitive, try mixing into a simple moisturiser.
