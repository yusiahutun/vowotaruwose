    The latest spin-off for the America's Got Talent series - AGT: Fantasy League -  is slated to premiere on January 1, 2024, New year's Day.

    With the big debut fast approaching, Heidi Klum headed back to Red Studios on Wednesday for another day of work alongside her colleagues in the judges panel: Howie Mandel, Simon Cowell, Mel B and host Terry Crews.

    And like she has done for the many years she's worked on the original AGT show, which began for her in season eight in 2013, the longtime supermodel dropped a number of poses as she made the walk to the entrance doors.

    In the first moments after being dropped off in front of the studio, the former catwalk queen stopped to show off some simple moves decked out in a skin-tight white minidress and a faded blue denim jacket

    In a couple of perfect matches to her minidress, Klum also wore white thigh-high Steve Madden boots and carried a large bag that likely had many of her essentials for the day, all while flashing her infectious smile.

    Heidi Klum, 50, put on her usual catwalk-like show upon arrival to the studio , only this time before another day shooting America's Got Talent: Fantasy League

    With the ground still wet from the rain that fell, the mother of four had to be careful while walking and posing in her heeled boots, which went off without a hitch.

    Rounding out her arrival ensemble, Klum also wore large round sunglasses, and had her blonde tresses styled long, straight and flowing down over her chest and to the middle of her back, with her trademark bangs and subtle center part.

    Klum went inside to begin her beauty preps for the day's shoot, which included changing into a pink shoulder-less ensemble.

    At one point, while the crowd was roaring with excitement in the studio, Klum decides to capture the moment and snap a photo of herself with her fellow three judges and host.

    'See you on January 1 for #AGT: Fantasy League,' she wrote in the caption, in a promotional push for the new show, which is the fourth in the AGT franchise.

    Following the original show, there's now been three spin-offs: AGT: The Champions, AGT: Extreme, AGT: All-Stars, and now AGT: Fantasy League. 

    For this latest edition, each judge chooses a roster of their favorite acting, picking from the winners, finalists, viral sensations and fan favorites from the AGT franchise.

    The process to form a team of talent has been compared to a fantasy sports draft.

    For the first time in the show's history, fans will be given the power to vote, via the America's Got Talent app, right from the start. 

    In another first, each judge will compete alongside the contestants and will mentor and guide the talent through the competition, all in hopes of helping shape the ultimate winner. 

    In a judge against judge formula, each will fill their fantasy teams with 10 different acts that are picked from the draft pool. 

    Mel B (born Melanie Janine Brown), 48, returns to the AGT judges' desk alongside Klum, executive producer Simon Cowell, 64, Howie Mandel, 67, and Terry Crews, 55, who's back as the host of the show.   

    America's Got Talent: Fantasy League premieres on Monday, January 1 at 8 p.m. ET on NBC, and will stream the next day on Peacock. 

    The main AGT series is set to return for season 19 in the summer of 2024 with Crews back as the host, along with Klum, Cowell, Mandel, and Sofia Vergara who are all expected to return as judges.
