    It seems everyone is in Miami for Art Basel this week—and everyone includes Kim and Khloé Kardashian.

    The SKIMS founder stepped out in the city last night with her younger sister in a look that gave a nod to both early-aughts hip-hop fashion and modern motocross style. It consisted of a tiny black custom Vex latex bandeau top; belted racer pants with blue, white, gray, and black stripes; and black leather over-the-knee heeled boots.

    Kim finished the look with a pair of oversized black sunglasses and pointy powder pink nails. She kept her glam casual, wearing her blonde straight hair down and opting for natural bronzy makeup and a matte pink lip.

    BACKGRID

    Khloé took a style cue from her former Balenciaga darling sister, stepping out in a black, sleeveless zip-up catsuit. The Good American cofounder carried a bedazzled silver clutch bag in the shape of an alien head, from Judith Leiber, and accessorized with Versace's VE2240 sunglasses in black with gold hardware. She, too, wore natural makeup and had her hair down for the night out.

    The sisters enjoyed dinner at Japanese steakhouse Gekkō, David Grutman and Bad Bunny's newest restaurant, prior to attending W Magazine and Burberry’s Art Basel celebration.

    Art Basel, the annual arts festival held in Miami, this year runs from December 1 to 3. Over the years, the festival has become an opportunity for fashion brands to showcase their collections and throw lavish beachfront parties with VIP guests. The Kardashian sisters—who both have their own clothing brands—are expected to make several appearances throughout their time in the city.
