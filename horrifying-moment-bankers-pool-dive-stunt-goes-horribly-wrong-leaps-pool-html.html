    This is the shocking moment a man leaps through a glass swimming pool panel in a stunt gone wrong at his friend's boozy 30th birthday bash. 

    Dimitry Prigorodov had invited guests to celebrate his birthday when his friend Cyril hurled himself through a window last week, hoping to dive straight into a swimming pool below. 

    But Cyril did not jump far enough and crashed into the glass and metal barrier surrounding the pool.

    Cyril hurls himself through a window, hoping to dive straight into a swimming pool below. He does not leap far enough though and crashes into a glass panel surround the pool

    He is seen diving head first out of the window aiming for the swimming pool below. 

    After colliding with the glass panel Cyril then fell into the swimming pool. 

    Dimitry, a banker from Moscow, said: ’It was my birthday and I rented a cottage to celebrate my anniversary.

    ‘We were celebrating and drinking alcohol.

    ‘It seemed to us that we could try and jump in principle.'

    Cyril ends up in the water but only after crashing through a glass panel causing ligament damage and a bruised chest

    Cyril, who had been invited by Dimitry to celebrate his birthday, clings to the side of the pool after his tumble. The group did not have to pay for the damage to the glass panel because of a mistake in the rental contract

    Unfortunately his stunt did not go as planned and Cyril suffered torn ligaments a bruised chest and has since had two operations to heal his injured hands and legs.

    His left arm and leg have stopped working.

    Despite Cyril's injuries, Dimitry posted the clip, which has become popular among his friends, on his social media and said 'it wasn’t for nothing’.

    Although the cottage where the stunt took place was rented, the group did not have to pay for the damage caused by hitting the glass panel as the rental contract had not been drafted correctly.
