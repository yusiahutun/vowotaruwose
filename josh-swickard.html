    Josh Swickard is an American actor who gained huge fame after appearing in Netflix’s Roped. He is also a model and recently got married to the American actress Lorynn York. The couple has shown great chemistry in the movie and Josh is also recognized for his performances such as in Roped, A California Christmas, and so on. Apart from this, he is also famous for playing Harrison Chase in the ABC soap opera, General Hospital. 

    Early Life 

    Josh Swickard was born on 4 July 1992, in Illinois. His father Bob is a pastor at St. Matthew’s United Methodist Church in Belleville and his mother Gina is a vocal coach. Josh is the second child of his loving and caring parents. He has three sisters, one elder and two younger than him. Elyssa, Kalia, and Elayna, respectively. He completed his schooling at Glenwood High school and finished his graduation from the College of DuPage, where he pursued accounting for 2 years. He also did modeling for 6 years in Chicago. Swickard is very fond of adventurous activities like hiking, and skiing, he is also passionate about musical instruments and knows how to play drums. He also enjoys dancing and other such activities.

    Josh Swickard Career

    Josh Swickard is overseen by Factor Chosen Chicago and Wilhelmina Models. He began his career through modeling in 2010, when he appeared in the Oh Lala magazine. He was on the cover of the same even in the year 2012. In 2013, he was on the cover of the magazine Q. He has also done modeling for famous and reputed companies like Men’s Health, Sports Illustrated, Guess, Jockey, Finish Line, and many others. He made his debut in the film industry in 2015, by appearing as a guest in one of the episodes of KC Undercover as a footballer.
