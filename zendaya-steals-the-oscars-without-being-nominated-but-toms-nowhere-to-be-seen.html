    Zendaya steals the Oscars without being nominated - but Tom's nowhere to be seen

    Zendaya has been serving us all the Red Carpet looks lately as she promotes her movie Dune 2, and so obviously she was going to slay the Oscars. She did just that, with her dress this year, leaving fans speechless.

    12 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Zendaya has been serving us all the Red Carpet looks lately as she promotes her movie Dune 2, and so obviously she was going to slay the Oscars. She did just that, with her dress this year, leaving fans speechless.

    The Hollywood star can do it all, as this year she served as a presenter for the Oscars ceremony serving the look of all looks. Everyone’s talking about Zendaya online, as they should, but they can’t help but notice her boyfriend Tom Holland was nowhere to be seen.

    The Euphoria star had all eyes on her as she glistened her way down the carpet. The pink and silver gown, which trailed down to the floor, featured a unique palm tree pattern.

    Underneath, the dress rocked a glittery black corset, as the rest of the dress elegantly sat on one shoulder.

    As the dress was a moment in itself, the 27-year-old opted for minimal accessories, leaving out the necklace but stunning in matching drop earrings, a ring, and a bracelet from Bulgari.

    She kept the vision with her short hair for the 96th Annual Academy Awards show with a side parting and a bounce sitting just above her shoulder. Old-style Hollywood eat your heart out!

    The look was once again in collaboration with her stylist Law Roach, and the two have quickly become one of the most iconic fashion duos of the moment.

    Zendaya was the talk of X, formerly known as Twitter, and it’s not hard to see why. “One thing about Law Roach .. he gon style Zendaya from the roota to the toota and she gone EAT it up every time,” wrote one user.

    Although she wasn’t up for an award this year, another user penned: “Zendaya should win an award for best standing,” we stand with that!

    “I’ll only comment for Zendaya and for Zendaya only she’s the fashion goddess if she was modeling they wouldn’t stand a chance,” a third commented.

    Others thought the look was so iconic, they photoshopped her in replace of the Oscars statue. We’re sure many would want to win that award!

    Tom Holland was nowhere to be seen

    We all know that Zendaya’s movie star boyfriend is her biggest supporter, leaving the cutest comments all over her Instagram.

    However, he was nowhere to be seen at the Academy Awards this year, as many thought he would be there to watch his partner present the Cinematography award.

    The celebrity couple keeps their relationship mostly on the down-low, so at this point, and unfortunately, we’ll have to carry on starving. But why wasn’t the Spider-Man star there?

    No need to worry, he’s still supporting Zendaya on the ‘gram but he was in London this weekend hosting a fundraising event for his charity, The Brother’s Trust.

    We’re manifesting a joint Red Carpet appearance for next year’s Oscars!
