    Ross Marquand’s Career: Movies, Family Guy

    After graduation, Ross Marquand moved to Los Angeles and quickly garnered attention in several film and television projects. In 2006, he first appeared the short Love Sick and the TV series documentary LA Forensics. In 2009 he had a recurring role as Siona’s Publicist in the TV series I <3 Vampires.

    Besides TV series, Marquand has starred in the movies Saturday Night Special, Kate Wakes, The Inappropriate Behavior of Our Alleged Loved Ones, Happy After, Slings & Arrows, The Congregation, Eden, Broken Roads, Sabbatical, Une Liberation, Down & Dangerous. His recent movies are Avengers: Infinity War(2018) as Red Skull and Avengers: Endgame(2019).

    Further, Ross also produced and starred in the TV series The Impression Guys and had a recurring role in the television series The Walking Dead in 2015.
