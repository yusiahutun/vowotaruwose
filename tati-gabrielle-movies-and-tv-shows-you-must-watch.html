    If you’re a fan of excellent acting, these Tati Gabrielle movies and TV shows should make your watchlist. The stunning actress was born Tatiana Gabrielle Hobson to an African-American father and a Korean-American mother in San Francisco, California, on January 25, 1996. At age three, she landed modeling jobs for department stores and began her career as a child star. 

    After debuting in the 2014 film “To Stay the Sword,” the talented star has had a rising career in Hollywood. Gabrielle’s critically lauded portrayal in “The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina” has made her a well-known face to Netflix viewers. 

    In addition, her most recent performances, such as her co-starring role with Tom Holland in the action-adventure movie “Uncharted” in 2022, have brought her enormous popularity and acclaim from viewers and fans. Gabrielle also made waves after getting cast as Jade in May 2023 in an untitled sequel to the 2021 “Mortal Kombat” film. 

    Tati Gabrielle Movies and TV Shows You Need To Watch

    Here are some must-see movies and TV shows starring Tati Gabrielle.

    Batman: The Doom That Came to Gotham (2023)

    In this 1920s-set animation, Bruce Wayne unintentionally releases an ancient evil, hastening his two-decade absence from Gotham City. Gabrielle portrays an original character, Kai Li Cain, a secondary protagonist Bruce Wayne takes under his wing. This film is one of the Tati Gabrielle movies and TV shows highlighting her talent and versatility. 

    As the voice of an original character, Gabrielle had to do more than introduce this new character to the DC animated universe. The stunning actress also had to make an impression. Fortunately, Gabrielle’s character, Kai Li Cain, became a fan favorite adored by critics and fans, earning her further recognition. 

    Kaleidoscope (2023)

    Like most Tati Gabrielle movies and TV shows, this Eric Garcia’s heist drama tested the stunning actress’s talent. The eight-part series centers on Leo Pap (Giancarlo Esposito) and his team, including Tati Gabrielle, who played Hannah Kim, as they attempt a $7 billion grand theft. Although the show debuted on Netflix in January 2023, it is unique for its scrambled sequence. 

    Gabrielle plays Hannah Kim, Leo Pap’s pregnant daughter and the head of digital security at SLS. Her character shows how effortlessly the talented actress can move between the high-stakes financial and low-lying criminal worlds. In addition, Gabrielle’s portrayal of Hannah Kim justifies the character’s powerful and sensitive sides.

    Uncharted (2022)

    While most Tati Gabrielle movies and TV shows highlight the actress’s heroic and modest sides, this film depicts her differently. “Uncharted” is the ideal movie to see the talented actress as a cold-blooded psychopath. She is essentially the film’s female lead and the main villain alongside Antonio Banderas’ Santiago Moncada. 

    In this Ruben Fleischer-directed film, Gabrielle plays the role of Jo Braddock, the leader of Moncada’s mercenaries who fights Nathan (Tom Holland) and Sully (Mark Wahlberg). Although the movie received mixed reactions from critics, it owes its success among viewers to the terrific cast, including Tom Holland, Mark Wahlberg, Sophia Ali, Antonio Banderas, and Tati Gabrielle.

    You (2021-Present)

    This psychological suspense television series debuted in September 2018. It centers on the relationship between a serial killer and a bookshop manager. Following Netflix’s decision to renew “You” for a third season in January 2020, Tati Gabrielle joined the show’s cast. She plays the main character of Marienne Bellamy, a librarian who keeps a close eye on the locals.

    Like in most Tati Gabrielle movies and TV shows, viewers like Gabrielle’s interpretation of her character, Marienne Bellamy. Her stellar performance gives the character a sympathetic and pleasant aura despite the show’s dark and powerful plot. Netflix announced in March 2023 that there will be a fifth season, which will also be the last. Hence, fans look forward to seeing the talented actress in the new season. 

    To Stay the Sword (2014)

    The list of some of the best Tati Gabrielle movies and TV shows is incomplete without “To Stay the Sword.” This film marks the talented actress’s debut in the movie business and is an excellent watch. Her film credit is under Tatiana Hobson in the short film, where she played the character Keating. Although not easy to find, the 18-minute short film marks the first step to Gabrielle’s successful career in Hollywood.

    The 100 (2017-2020)

    This sci-fi follows post-apocalyptic survivors from a space habitat, the Ark, who return to Earth almost a century after a devastating nuclear apocalypse. Although the series premiered on The CW on March 19, 2014, Gabrielle debuted in the fourth season. Hence, she appeared in 23 episodes up till the seventh season. 

    Gabrielle appeared as Gaia, Indra’s estranged daughter. Gaia serves as a Flamekeeper, guarding and preserving the sacred Flame, which houses the souls of former commanders. Although most viewers think Gaia’s plot in the series was undeveloped, Gabrielle’s captivating and powerful performance makes it a must-watch.
