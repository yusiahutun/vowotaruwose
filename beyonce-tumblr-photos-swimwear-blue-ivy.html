      Beyoncé has shared enviable photos on her Tumblr page in which the queen of pop looks more stunning than ever.

      Posting the holiday snaps under her "My Life" section, the mother-of-one, 32, is seen modelling a collection of statement swimsuits.In one photo, Beyoncé smoulders at the camera in a striking red ruffled one piece. The singer poses side on, flaunting her famous curves and highlighting the swimsuit's low-cut front and trendy back with draping gold necklaces.

      Beyoncé adds to her boho-chic look wearing her blonde waves loose, matching red lipstick and gold chain headbands.

      In another snapshot, Beyoncé sports an equally racy swimsuit featuring a triangular cut out middle section that shows off her flat stomach. The Grammy-Award winning artist playfully pouts at the camera and hides behind her dark oversized shades.

      Showcasing a third swimsuit, Beyoncé changed into an edgier white one-piece featuring a tiger's face emblazoned across her figure.

      The doting mother also shared photos of her close-knit family, including one of two-year-old Blue Ivy from the back, playing in the sand in a matching red two-piece.

      Beyoncé, who has been married to legendary rapper Jay-Z for nearly six years, also posted one of her husband from the back, stretching his arms out and looking up to the paradisiacal sky.

      The photos, showing off Beyoncé's flawless figure, come just days after the star reportedly spent £700 at a McDonald's in Manchester, England.

      A hungry fan visited the fast food chain on Tuesday night, only to discover that Beyoncé had almost cleared out the joint.

      "I'd stopped off on my way home from the theatre to buy a cheeseburger at McDonald's as I was feeling peckish," local resident Helen Nugent told .

      "The lovely girl at the till looked a bit shell-shocked and I soon found out why. She told me 'Oh my god, I've just done an order for £700 for Beyoncé's 11 tour buses.'"She then went on to tell me, 'I made 50 portions of large fries and 22 boxes of Chicken McNuggets. 22! And 26 Big Macs! There are a lot of dancers on her tour, you'd think they eat healthily. Mind you, they did order a lot of salad. "Beyoncé is currently in the UK performing for her Mrs Carter world tour.
