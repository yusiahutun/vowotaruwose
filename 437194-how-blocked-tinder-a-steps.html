    Tinder rolled out Block Contacts as an additional feature on its application and allowed everyone to use it and update whenever they desired. Since the application is connected to one's phone number, whoever is on the blocked list will not see you on Tinder. So, how do you do it?

    • Go to the Tinder settings page and click on Block Contacts.
    • Allow Tinder access to your phone’s contact.
    • Select the contact you want to block or avoid on Tinder.
    • Once you find a name you want to block, please select it and hit the block button. They are then added to the blocked list.

    You can also manually block someone if you don't have their contact on your phone using the + feature in the Blocked Contact section.

    • Open the Tinder app and access the Blocked Contact feature.
    • Hit the + button at the top right section of your screen to manually put the phone number of the person you intend to block.
    • Once you have added them, click done, and the person will never show up in your Tinder deck.
