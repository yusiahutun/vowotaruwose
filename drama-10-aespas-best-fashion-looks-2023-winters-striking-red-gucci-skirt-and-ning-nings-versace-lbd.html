    Drama: 10 of Aespas best fashion looks in 2023, from Winters striking red Gucci skirt and Ning

    The group, composed of Karina, Winter, Giselle and Ning Ning, has not only achieved remarkable success in the music industry, but has also solidified its presence in the fashion world. To showcase the ladies impeccable style, here is a compilation of Aespas best looks of 2023.

    26 May 2024, 00:00

    The group, composed of Karina, Winter, Giselle and Ning Ning, has not only achieved remarkable success in the music industry, but has also solidified its presence in the fashion world.

    The foursome are brand ambassadors for luxury labels such as Chopard and Givenchy. Additionally, during this year’s fashion weeks, they were spotted at the coveted front-row seats at shows like Givenchy and Thom Browne.

    To showcase the ladies’ impeccable style, here is a compilation of Aespa’s best looks of 2023.

    Moving’s breakout star Han Hyo-joo did quiet luxury before it was cool

    1. Aespa’s Drama look

    For their latest release, the girls are showing off their edgier side in pieces from brands like Mugler, Rick Owens, Dion Lee and more.

    2. Ning Ning’s Versace LBD

    Aespa’s youngest member Ning Ning looked stunning in Versace’s Butterfly Medusa dress as she attended the brand’s pop-up in Seoul in July this year.

    3. Ning Ning’s ethereal Loewe ‘fit

    The 21-year-old looked dreamy indeed in Loewe’s Maruja Mallo-print maxi dress.

    Will Blackpink survive the 7-year curse, like BTS and Girls’ Generation?

    4. Karina’s classy Bottega Veneta moment

    When Karina attended a Bottega Veneta pop-up store event in Seoul, she wore an asymmetric dress from the brand and carried its classic Andiamo bag.

    5. Karina’s tailored Thom Browne ensemble

    Aespa’s leader, Karina sat front row for the Thom Browne debut at Paris Haute Couture Week this year. She completed her tailored Thom Browne look with the brand’s signature Hector bag.

    6. Winter’s stunning Gucci outfit

    Aespa’s lead vocalist, Winter, joined a star-studded guest list of celebrities at Gucci’s cruise show in South Korea earlier this year. Her bolder than usual, cropped Gucci knit and red leather skirt combo had many fans in awe.

    Style queens: Blackpink’s best fashion looks during the Born Pink World Tour

    7. Winter’s all-red schoolgirl look

    The 22-year-old gave us an iconic red-and-black moment in this Polo Ralph Lauren look complete with the brand’s Polo ID suede shoulder bag.

    8. Giselle’s chic all-brown look at Paris Fashion Week

    Japanese-Korean Giselle stunned in an all-brown ensemble from Acne Studios for the brand’s spring/summer 2024 show in Paris.

    9. Giselle’s spicy look for Ottolinger

    For Ottolinger’s show at Paris Fashion Week 2024, the 23-year-old rapper wore a red set and the brand’s signature baguette bag.

    The girls owned the schoolgirl aesthetic during their Berlin show as part of their Synk:Hyperline world tour that ended in September this year.
