    TikTok Says These Perfumes Are Exact Dupes of Cloud by Ariana Grande

    Ariana Grande’s 2018 released Cloud perfume is almost as popular as the pop star is. While many perfumes have people saying “thank u, next,” after just a few months of wear, beauty lovers can’t seem to get enough of the addicting creamy, sweet scent that features top notes of lavender, pear, and whipped cream, and dries down to a woody base. Many fans even go so far as to compare (and prefer) it to the iconic Maison Francis Kurkdjian Baccarat Rouge 540 Eau de Parfum. And though it costs way less than the MFK scent at just $65, TikTok loves finding good dupes that won’t break the bank.

    Here at StyleCaster, we’re all about saving you money. That’s why we’ve scoured hundreds of clips on the clock app, hunting for the most recommended dupes of Ari’s famous fragrance. We’ve included picks for every budget, with the most affordable option clocking in at just $20 — which is more than 70 percent off the OG cost. With all the money you’ll be saving on Cloud, you can afford to try even more dupes of your fave designer fragrances!

    So, what are you waiting for? Keep scrolling to see the fragrance finds that TikTok has deemed as being the perfect Ariana Grande Cloud perfume dupes.

    • Sol de Janeiro Brazilian Crush Cheirosa ’68 Beija Flor

      This Brazilian brand needs no introduction. Their Bum Bum Cream and Body Mist are the stuff of legends. But this newer fragrance from Sol de Janeiro deserves the same amount of hype. It’s fruity, fresh, and has a hint of vanilla. What more could you ask for in a perfume?

    • MIX:BAR Coconut Palm Hair & Body Mist

      $16.99$19.9915% Off

      MIX:BAR’s Coconut Palm scent is a favorite dupe for many, as it’s super easy to find at your local Target. TikToker @rocio.roses did a sniff test video of this perfume along with a price comparison. The 1.7 ounce version of Cloud costs $55, while the same size of Coconut Palm is priced at just $19.99. And for a limited time, you can take 15 percent off of the MIX:BAR brand, taking the price down to $16.99 — such a steal!

    • Pacifica Beauty Dream Moon Spray Perfume

      Fragrance content creator @paulreactss put us (and 1.3 million fans) on Pacifica’s Beauty Dream Moon Spray Perfume in a clip from last September, and we haven’t been the same since. “It was literally spot on, to the ‘T,'” Paul says of the perfume, before declaring its Cloud dupe-dom.

    • Skylar Boardwalk Delight

      Skylar’s Boardwalk Delight has that same creamy cotton candy quality that Cloud does, thanks to the shared coconut milk note. If you get the rollerball (which our editors say lasts up to a year), it’ll only set you back $30.
