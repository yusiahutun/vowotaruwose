    Sydney Sweeney continued her style streak yesterday with a red-hot look perfect for the holiday season.

    The Euphoria star made an appearance Tuesday on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon in a cherry-red minidress featuring a balconette bra and a sculpted corset bodice. The mini was seemingly strapless from the front, though short sleeves came up from the back. The skirt, meanwhile, swept up on one side and was accented by a large rosette.

    Sweeney paired the dress simply with glossy pumps in the same red shade, a blood-red manicure, and a bold red lip. She wore large silver drop earrings by Mejuri, some silver rings, and shimmery silver eye shadow.

    NBC//Getty Images

    NBC//Getty Images

    Sweeney was on The Tonight Show to talk about her new romantic comedy, Anyone but You, which she stars in with Glen Powell. The costars attended the premiere of the film in New York on Monday—and looked incredible posing together on the red carpet.

    Sweeney had a busy day of press for the movie yesterday, and changed into three different looks—plus the red minidress—for her appearances. One ensemble consisted of a white sequin suit composed of a vest and wide-leg pants, and paired with black platform peep-toe heels. Another was a romantic baby-pink minidress with a matching coat and velvet heels. And the third was a sleek all-black daytime suit composed of high-waisted pants and a cutout crop top with a sharp collar that doubled as a halterneck strap.
