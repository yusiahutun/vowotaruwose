    Kylie Jenner has outdone herself with her couture Halloween look.

    The style star, who is known for her history of pop culture-themed costumes, celebrated Halloween weekend by sharing an extravagant photoshoot spotlighting her Bride of Frankenstein costume. To pay homage to the 1935 film, Jenner paired her impressive makeup recreation with a custom Jean Paul Gaultier couture gown by Glenn Martens.

    The piece includes a curve-hugging silhouette, long sleeves, and a high straight neckline, and the semi-sheer bodice is covered in dozens of strips of fabric threaded through grommets that run down the length of the dress. The luxury house also made matching fabric-covered heels and white gloves.

    The Kylie Cosmetics founder finished the look with a glamorous spin on the classic Bride of Frankenstein makeup, including elongated eyebrows with pointed ends and a dark lip, while wearing her hair in a high curly bouffant with two white streaks along each side.

    Kylie Jenner//Instagram

    The couture gown was one of several costumes she wore for the black-and-white photo shoot. The star also wore a flowing white gown paired with nude mummy-style opera gloves, in a similar style to the original film's bridal look. A close-up shot from the traditional look also showcased two dark stitches "holding" her head to her neck.

    Kylie Jenner//Instagram

    Jenner also changed into a sexy two-piece outfit, comprised of a strapless tube top and connected opera gloves, constructed to look like unraveling white bandages. In addition to posing bottomless, in a creepy shot where she screams while holding a large knife, she paired the top with a black maxi-length skirt tied at the hip.

    Kylie Jenner//Instagram

    Kylie Jenner//Instagram

    She finished off the series of posts with a behind-the-scenes video from the fashion shoot, soundtracked with the horror remix of "I Got 5 On It" from the Jordan Peele film Us, for the full spooky effect.
