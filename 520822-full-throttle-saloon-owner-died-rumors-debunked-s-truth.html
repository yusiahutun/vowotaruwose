    Who is Michael Ballard?

    The American celebrity was born on 9 December 1965 in Trimble, Tennessee, United States, meaning he is 57 years old as of 2023. Ballard has not revealed the details about his parents and siblings. He was a naughty kid while growing up, so the local police nicknamed him Little Al Capone.

    Michael commenced his career in the cellular phone business, owning a Cellular Plus company. In the late 90's, he sold his company and acquired 30 acres of land in Sturgis, South Dakota.

    The successful entrepreneur then constructed the Full Throttle Saloon, an outdoor bar and recreation facility. Full Throttle Saloon is the largest biker bar in the world. It has several restaurants, a tattoo parlour, stores, two stages for performances, a wrestling ring, a zip line course, 200 cabins, and parking lots to accommodate everyone.

    Full Throttle Saloon owner died rumours debunked

    In 2016, rumours emerged that Michael Ballard had died, and many of his fans sought to know the Full Throttle Saloon owner cause of death. However, it was a case of mistaken identity where someone else with the same name died.

    The false information made many believe that the Full Throttle Saloon owner died in real life. However, Michael later took to social media to dispel the rumours and reassure his followers that he was still alive.

    Why did Full Throttle Saloon get cancelled?

    In 2009, the saloon became the subject of the truTV series Full Throttle Saloon. The reality show premiered on truTV on 10 November 2009 and ran for six seasons up to 2015.

    What happened to owners of Full Throttle Saloon?
