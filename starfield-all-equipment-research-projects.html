    Starfield offers many research projects to improve your gears. Here are all Equipment Research Projects in Starfield.

    Equipment research projects are a key part of upgrading and customizing your gear in Starfield. By completing research at specialized labs, you can unlock powerful new mods that augment your spacesuits, helmets, and packs.

    Equipment research projects are unlockable mods that are focused on augmenting your armor and gear. You can use Research Labs to complete specialized projects that provide you with the knowledge to create helmet mods, spacesuit mods, and pack mods.

    For example, helmet mods may improve detection range or elemental damage resistance. Spacesuit mods can bolster armor stats or increase sprint duration. Pack mods may expand inventory capacity or provide other utility effects.

    How to Unlock Equipment Research Projects?

    There are three Equipment Research trees in Starfield – Helmet Mods, Spacesuit Mods, and Pack Mods. Each tree has three tiers of research projects that must be unlocked sequentially.

    To unlock the next tier in a research tree, you must first complete the project in the preceding tier. For example, to unlock Helmet Mods 2, you must first finish the Helmet Mods 1 project.

    How to Complete Equipment Research Projects

    If you want to complete Equipment Research Projects, here’s a step-by-step process for you:

    • Locate a Research Lab at an outpost, settlement, or on your ship.
    • Access the Research Lab terminal and select the “Equipment” category.
    • Choose an equipment research project you want to work on. Take note of required resources and skills.
    • Gather the necessary resources by mining, collecting, purchasing, etc.
    • If needed, allocate crew members with the required skills for the project.
    • Interact with the Research Lab terminal again and select your desired project. Contribute your resources.
    • Wait for the project duration to elapse. Optionally assign crew to speed it up.
    • When the project completes, the new mod recipes will be available at Workbenches.
    • Visit a Spacesuit Workbench and craft your newly unlocked mods! Equip them to customize your gear.
    • Repeat for additional research projects to unlock further upgrade tiers.

    The mod system encourages experimentation and thoughtful gear selection. What do you want to maximize? Exploration ability, combat power, or resource gathering? The choices are yours, so research wisely and mod strategically!
