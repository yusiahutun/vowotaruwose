    The former Basketball player, Rachel DeMita, is well-appreciated with her hosting skills in NBA 2KTV. She left the show in November 2018, but still, her legacy lives on the show among the audiences.

    The elegant and charming young lady made an impressive profile in the sporting world. Moreover, with her personality, her followers were eager to know about her dating life. But is she dating or concerned in her career?

    For those who are seeking the unsolved question of her personal life, you are welcome to spend your time!

    Rumored NBA Boyfriend

    The beautiful former basketball player, Rachel DeMita, was rumored to be dating a man of the same profession back in 2014. She was rumored to be dating the American basketball player, Kevin Durant, and had been seeing each other since his birthday in New York City.

    They were noticed leaving the party together and were spotted kissing.

    However, the basketball-loving pair never officiated themselves as a couple. Even if the pair were dating, they are not together anymore as Kevin Durant got a new girlfriend, Cass Anderson, who sits on a Wifey section during his match.

    As for the model, Rachel remained far from dating but expressed her opinion and past dating experiences on her YouTube channel. 

    In a YouTube video titled STORYTIME! (MY AWKWARD VALENTINE'S DAY) // Rachel DeMita, the reality star revealed her awkward Valentine's day story. She explained she planned her first Valentine's with her high school boyfriend.

    Like every teenage girl, she bought gifts and chocolates and was excited to spend with him. However, her then-sweetheart did not wish her, and she ended up giving away all the gifts to her girlfriends.

    But at the end of her day, Rachel's boyfriend surprised her with balloons and a huge teddy bear. Despite all, their relationship could not long last.

    Apart from that, she expressed more of her opinions in a YouTube video titled Marriage And Kids, published in February 2018. She was asked whether she was to date an NBA player or not and the reasons for it. And she just ignored the question and told 

    "I will never answer it"

    Similarly, in the same video, she was asked what she would name her baby. In which, she revealed that for a baby, she needed to be married first. And to get married she needs to have a boyfriend which she does not have for now. She further hinted that she is single and is not interested in any dating relationship.

    Well, Rachel took to Twitter with a tweet questioning about a boyfriend back on 10th April 2018. But she did not answer her question leaving her fans more curious to know about her relationship status.

    Rachel DeMita's boyfriend tweet in April 2018 (Photo: Rachel A Demita's twitter)

    Similarly, her fans are curious to know about her boyfriend. Since she is a vegan and one of her fans also asked if her boyfriend needs to be vegan too? And in the video, Do I Only Date Vegans? that dated back on 28th April 2018, she revealed the boy she is dating should be vegan. But he should respect the fact that she is vegan.

    Dating NBA Player Confirmed

    After disclosing the quality of her boyfriend, the -29-year-old host finally found the one whom she is flaunting all over her Instagram.

    The talk show host started dating the NBA player, Andre Roberson, since 2018. She confirmed her rumored relationship with the Oklahoma City Thunder guard by sharing the adorable image on the Instagram story in June 2018. In the shared account, the heart emojis floated around her and Andre's head.

    They have been rumored to have been dating after Rachel tagged Andre's Instagram handle in a post on 13th June 2018.

    Similarly, Andre also gave weight to the newly blossomed relationship as he posted the pictures with the 2K TV host on Instagram on 24th July 2018. Following the post, numerous followers gave the best regards to the couple.

    As of 2019, the pair's relationship is going stronger than ever. They even spent Valentine's day together on 14th February that year and shared the pictures among their fans a few days later.

    The TV icon could be seen donning a beautiful designer black dress for her special night with a rose in her hand. Yes, she had spent perfect and typical Valentine's day with her muse that day.

     Rachel DeMita and boyfriend Andre Roberson on 14th February 2019 (Photo: Rachel DeMita's Instagram)

    The pair first confirmed their relationship in the summer of 2018, and after wishing her beau a happy birthday, their status as a couple started becoming evident.

    The actress, who stands at the height of 5ft 8 inch (1.72m), was an All-American basketball player and earned a full-ride division 1 scholarship to Old Dominion University in 2008. She never thought of pursuing Basketball as her career in the future.
