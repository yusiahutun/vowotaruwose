    No matter the season, denim is always the perfect go-to clothing item. After all, there are so many different styles, it can be dressed up or down and jeans pretty much go with anything. One of the trickiest types of denim to figure out, though, is definitely ripped jeans. Figuring out how to wear ripped jeans without someone’s parent asking you if you paid extra for the holes in your pants (My dad is guilty of this every! single! time!) can be difficult. Have no fear, though, because we turned to the experts— bloggers, influencers and street style queens—to get some major ripped jean outfit inspiration.

    Related Stories

    There’s no need to reserve your beloved ripped jeans for days when you’re just hanging around the house or going out to a bar on a weekend. It’s totally possible to make ripped jeans look super chic, and we found so many ideas to help you get started. Whether you’re trying to get your ripped jeans approved for casual Fridays at work or just want to amp up your weekend street style, we got you. Try pairing your favorite ripped jeans with a flow-y blouse and heels. Or, add a blazer and some booties to your ripped jeans look. A faux leather jacket and sneakers is also a fun way to style your ripped jeans. What we’re saying is: The possibilities are endless.

    Below, you’ll find 29 different ripped jeans outfit ideas. Next time you’re searching through your closet trying to figure out what exactly to pair with your ripped jeans, take a gander at this list for some inspiration. You’re sure to find an outfit that ignites some excitement in you and gives you the perfect idea for your next ensemble.
