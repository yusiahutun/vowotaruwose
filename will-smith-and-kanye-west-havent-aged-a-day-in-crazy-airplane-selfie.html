    Will Smith and Kanye West haven't aged a day in 'crazy' airplane selfie

    Kanye West and Will Smith have been spotted together back from the Middle East, and fans cant believe the selfie. Both parties have had their fair share of drama over the last few years, but judging by this new pic, the stress hasnt made them age a day.

    21 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Kanye West and Will Smith have been spotted together back from the Middle East, and fans can’t believe the selfie. Both parties have had their fair share of drama over the last few years, but judging by this new pic, the stress hasn’t made them age a day.

    The celebrity pals seemed to be across the pond for different reasons, but crossed paths on the plane ride home. Will Smith performed at the Soundstorm music festival in Saudi Arabia alongside DJ Jazzy Jeff, with his son Jaden in attendance. It’s unclear what Kanye was doing, however, he’s been spending quite a bit of time there over the past few months to work on his album.

    Will Smith and Kanye West pose for airplane selfie

    In the photo, now circulating social media, the Fresh Prince of Bel-Air star can be seen smiling for the camera, as he holds his arm out to take a selfie with the rapper.

    Kanye West, however, doesn’t seem to hold the same smile, although we all know it’s a rarity to find him smiling in a pic, so we wouldn’t take it personally if we were Will.

    It’s said the two were on board First Class on an Emirates Flight. It’s unclear how involved the two were with each other, but it looks like they’re on good terms.

    Both looked comfortable on board, as the Runaway singer wore a black hoodie with his hood up, whereas the movie star opted for a blue jumper.

    As the photo circulates social media, fans took to X, formerly known as Twitter to comment on the ‘crazy’ pic.

    “You could tell me this picture was from 2009 and I’d believe it,” wrote one.

    Another said: “Crazy pic”.

    “Never seen Kanye look so much like Kanye,” tweeted another.

    It’s looks like the 55-year-old is catching up with all his celeb pals as of late. Smith recently sent the internet crazy when he posted an adorable clip with Johnny Depp.

    Kanye delays album release

    Although it seems like he may have gone to Saudi Arabia to work on his album, Ye may not have been working hard enough as he delayed the release.

    His promised joint album with Ty Dolla $ign was meant to be released on December 15, however, fans were disappointed when the tracks weren’t on streaming services.

    It’s now meant to drop on New Year’s Eve, so fans will have to wait an extra two weeks.
