    10 Mar 2024, 00:00

    A Brooklyn mother has been sentenced to 17 years in prison for beating her four-year-old son to death with a broomstick.

    Zarah Coombs was sentenced on Wednesday, after pleading guilty in January to charges of manslaughter and endangerment of a child. 

    When she is eventually released from prison, the 28-year-old will be on probation for five years. 

    Brooklyn mother Zarah Coombs, 28, was sentenced to 17 years in prison on Wednesday for the January 2017 death of her son Zamair, age four
