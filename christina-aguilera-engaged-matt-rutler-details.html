      "Matt spent over a year designing the ring with the ring designer to represent their three-year journey together as a couple," an insider told .

      "He has been planning this for a while. "He took two of Christina's favourite rings to design something that incorporated [both designs] into one, similar to both their lives coming together as one."

      Christina's tweet was swiftly retweeted more than 5,000 times and prompted a host of congratulations from her followers. 

      Matt and Christina met on the set of the 2010 movie . At the time, the mother-of-one was in the middle of a painful breakup with her ex-husband Jordan Bratman. 

      Christina and Jordan have a son Max Liron Bratman together. 

      Over the past three years Matt has become close to Christina's six-year-old Max and the trio live together in Los Angeles.
