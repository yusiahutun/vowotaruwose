    Historical significance

    Ambattur has a long and illustrious history that dates back to ancient times. It was once ruled by the Chola dynasty and played a crucial role in trade and commerce during that era.

    The name Ambattur

    The name Ambattur is believed to have been derived from the Tamil word “Ambalattu,” meaning the place of the goddess. It refers to the presence of an ancient temple dedicated to the goddess Kottur Amman in the area.

    Industrial hub

    Ambattur is renowned for its industrial prowess and is home to one of the largest industrial estates in South Asia, spanning over 1,400 acres. It houses numerous manufacturing units, including textile mills, engineering firms, and automobile factories.
