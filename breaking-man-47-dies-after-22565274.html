    Man, 47, dies after roof of house collapses into bedroom in Bradford

    A 47-year-old man has died after the roof of a house collapsed in Bradford on Sunday morning. Emergency services were called to Knight's Fold in the West Yorkshire city at about 5am this morning to reports the roof of a property had collapsed.

    19 Mar 2024, 00:00

    A 47-year-old man has died after the roof of a house collapsed in Bradford on Sunday morning.

    Emergency services were called to Knight's Fold in the West Yorkshire city at about 5am this morning to reports the roof of a property had collapsed.

    A man and woman had both suffered serious injuries after debris from the roof fell into a bedroom.

    The man suffered fatal injuries while the woman, 28, is being treated in hospital for non-life-threatening injuries.

    Three children inside the property were unharmed and taken out of the house, West Yorkshire Police said.

    The road has been closed and will remain that way for the rest of the day while engineers and emergency services carry out their work.

    Detective Inspector Claudine Binns of Bradford District CID, said: "This has clearly been a tragic incident in which a man has lost his life and a woman has received some serious injuries.

    "Work is ongoing to make the building safe and we are in the early stages of a full investigation with the local authority to determine exactly how this fatal incident took place.

    "A full file will be prepared for the coroner.

    "The victim's family will be rehoused and authorities are working to support them in any way we can.

    "Road closures will be in place at the scene throughout the day as structural engineers, police and fire services continue our work there."

    Knight's Fold is located in east Bradford.
