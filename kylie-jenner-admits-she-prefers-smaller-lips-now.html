      Kylie Jenner is renowned for her full, plump lips, so the 18-year-old surprised the beauty world on Tuesday when she admitted that she wants "small lips". This marks a significant change of heart for the star, who has previously turned to make-up tricks and temporary lip fillers to enhance her pout.

      Kylie Jenner has admitted she wants her lips "to look small"

      "You know what's weird? I've been into small lips lately," Kylie confessed during a beauty tutorial on her website with renowned make-up artist Joyce Bonelli. "Like, I want them to look small."

      While Kylie admitted during an episode of her family's hit reality TV show that she used temporary lip fillers to help achieve her plump-lipped look, she has always remained adamant that she would avoid a permanent change.

      "I would recommend that anyone who gets it done go for a filler that lasts only two to four months," she told the . "It's annoying to keep going back, but you have the option of stopping it."

      Mother Kris Jenner also opened up about her daughter's plastic surgery, revealing that she was initially "really upset" as she never gave Kylie permission to undergo the procedure.

      The young star is renowned for her regular makeovers

      "I got really upset, and then it was done, and I had to let it go," the 59-year-old matriarch told. "Once that was done, it's just my kid. I love my kid. We make mistakes."
