    Profession

    Alma Wahlberg worked as a bank clerk and nurse aide. She began her career as a clerk in a local bank. Later, she got another job as a nurse assistant, where she helped in providing medical assistance to the sick.

    In 2011, she began her on-screen career by appearing on Anderson Live. Three years later, she was featured on another reality show, Wahlburgers. She later got into the business by opening restaurants with her sons. They co-owned two restaurants, Alma Nove and Wahlburgers.

    Alma Wahlberg's family

    Elaine was a mother of nine children. She first got married to Donald Wahlberg in 1965 but divorced in 1982. Donald was a delivery driver and an army veteran who served during the Korean War. He died in 2008 in Dorchester.

    Donald and Elaine had nine children. They had three daughters and six sons. They are Arthur, Paul, Jim, Robert, Donnie, Mark, Michelle, Debbie and Tracey. Their daughter Debbie died in 2003.

    Alma Elaine married her second husband, Mark Conroy, in 1984. They were seeing each other for some time, and their romantic relationship culminated in a beautiful wedding. The couple tied the knot at a private party and in the presence of her family and close friends.
