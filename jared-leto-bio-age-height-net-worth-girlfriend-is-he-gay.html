    Jared Leto is a popular, multi-talented American actor, singer, songwriter, and director. He has received much recognition for his dedication to the roles he takes on. Before receiving recognition, he began his career by appearing on television shows and taking on small-part roles in films. His acting achievements are always breathtaking, thanks to the research he does for his roles, which always seems to pay off.

    Biography & Age

    Jared Leto was born on December 26, 1971, in Bossier City Louisiana. His mother, Constance Leto, is descended from the Cajun. Not much is known about his father except that he divorced his (Jared) mother when Jared and his brother – Shannon – were very young. Even his family name – Leto – is his stepfather’s name, not his father’s. His biological father remarried after his divorce from Constance Leto and had two sons from his second marriage. He later committed suicide when Jared was only eight years old.

    Jared’s maternal grandfather was in the Air Force, so the family traveled a lot. When he was young, his mother convinced him and his brother to become involved in the arts. He grew up around painters, writers, artists, singers, and actors, who in the long run inspired him to act and sing as well. His first musical instrument was a broken piano, which he began to play at a very young age.

    Jared dropped out of school in the 10th grade but later continued his studies at Emerson Preparatory School in Washington D.C. He studied at the University of the Arts in Philadelphia before moving to the School of Visual Arts in New York City, where he became interested in filmmaking. As a student, Jared Leto wrote a short film, Crying Joy, in which he also played the leading role.

    Jared Leto is also a founding member and lead singer of the American rock band Thirty Seconds To Mars.

    Is Jared Leto Gay?

    Speculation about Jared’s sexuality began to rise when he convincingly played a transsexual woman in the critically acclaimed Dallas Buyers Club. However, it is common practice for the media to often speculate about celebrity sexuality, so this came as no surprise. But for the record: Jared Leto is indeed heterosexual.
