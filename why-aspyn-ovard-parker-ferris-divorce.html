    If you’ve been following their relationship on YouTube for the past decade, you may be wondering why Aspyn Ovard and Parker Ferris are divorcing after eight years of marriage and what happened to the YouTuber couple’s seemingly solid relationship.

    Ovard, an influencer with more than two million followers on YouTube, and Ferris married on October 23, 2015, in Utah. “I have a wife! She has a husband!” Ferris said in a video shared on Ovard’s YouTube at the time. “I’m sure it will hit me a little later once we actually get situated, living together, normal married life.” Ovard was 19 at the time, while Ferris was 20.

    Their first child, a daughter named Cove, was born in 2019. “36 weeks! Cove came at 38 so we could have a new baby in 2 weeks 🤓😋,” Ovard captioned an Instagram post at the time. Their second child, a daughter named Lola, was born in 2021.

    For almost a decade, Ovard and Ferris shared their lives and relationship with her two-plus million subscribers on YouTube. Everything seemed fine until April 2024 when news broke that Ovard had filed for divorce from Ferris the same day she announced the birth of their third child. So why did Aspyn Ovard and Parker Ferris divorce? Read on for what we know so far about Aspyn Ovard‘s divorce ahead.

    Why did Aspyn Ovard and Parker Ferris divorce?

    Why did Aspyn Ovard and Parker Ferris divorce? Ovard filed for divorce from Ferris after eight years of marriage on April 1, 2024 in Salt Lake City, Utah, according to documents obtained by People. Ovard filed for divorce the same day she announced the birth of her and Ferris’ third child on Instagram and TikTok. “sweet baby was ready to be born i guess 👶🏼,” Ovard captioned a video announcing the birth of the baby, who was born early. On the video, she wrote, “but i’m only 34 weeks… she’s not ready. just trust me you’ll be fine.”

    Ovard also shared a TikTok video of Ferris pointing at her baby bump in the hospital bed while lip-syncing the SpongeBob quote, “My friend’s in there.” In the clip, Ferris could be seen wearing her wedding band, though Ovard isn’t wearing hers.

    In another Tiktok video, Ovard explained how she was in “shock” because of how early her child was born. “So I just had a baby. I feel like I’m in shock because she’s super early. My brain is just not processing what’s happening. We came in last night. I think we probably got here at like, I don’t know maybe 11 or 12. The baby was born at 6 a.m. and is in the NICU right now. She’s doing really well, but she’s super early, so she’s going to be there for a minute, so it’s definitely a weird experience to not have my baby on my chest right now,” she said. “I think I’m going to get moved to the mama baby unit soon and then after that, I can go up and be with her. I’m just so confused how I literally just had a baby.”

    So why did Aspyn Ovard and Parker Ferris divorce? As of writing, Ovard hasn’t revealed the reason for her and Ferris’ divorce. However, fans speculate that the divorce had been in the works for a while after Ovard changed the name of her family YouTube channel to just her name six months prior to her divorce filing. She explained in a YouTube video at the time that she changed the title of the channel because she doesn’t show her children in her videos anymore and “Parker is always with them,” which is why he isn’t in a lot of her videos either. Fans also noticed around the time that Ferris removed “husband” and “father” from his Instagram biography. Ferris has alos disabled Instagram comments after news of his and Ovard’s divorce.

    Reddit users have also resurfaced a YouTube video with Ovard and Ferris from 2023, in which Ovard explains why she isn’t interest in renewing her vows with Ferris. “I feel like the actual vow renewal, I wouldn’t care to do because people just don’t even keep vows. Like I don’t even remember what I said,” Ovard said in the video, which was titled “a q&a where we probably spill too much tea and also got in a fight.”
