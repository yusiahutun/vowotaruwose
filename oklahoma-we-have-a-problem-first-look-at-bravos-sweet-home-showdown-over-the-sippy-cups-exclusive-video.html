    "Sweet Home Oklahoma" stars Jennifer Welch and Lee Murphy confront Angie “Pumps” Sullivan over her parenting style in this week's episode of the Bravo reality series.

    The three ladies are dining when Sullivan's phone starts beeping because her kids are trying to track her down using the Find my iPhone app. Welch and Murphy thought the "ridiculous" moment was the perfect opportunity to address the larger issue with Sullivan's tendency of being a "helicopter mom": the sippy cups.

    TooFab's exclusive clip features the ladies' showdown over the sippy cup saga plaguing the teenagers.
