    The Kardashians are once again making another TikTok microtrend go mainstream. This time around, it’s Khloé taking the “mob wife” aesthetic for a spin.

    The Good American founder showed off her take on the fad yesterday, with an array of sultry photos posted to her Instagram. Posing in a rocky, wintry landscape, Kardashian looks unfazed in black snow boots and a tiny tan bikini bearing Gucci’s iconic monogram pattern.

    Tempted as you may be to categorize her risqué wintry look as “snow bunny,” her long fur coat and matching floppy fur hat cement the outfit’s entry into mob wife territory. “It’s giving mob wife vibes,” Kardashian even wrote in the caption. “Faux mob wife of course.”

    What does it even mean to dress up as mob wife? Writer Emilia Petrarca broke down the trend for Harper’s Bazaar, noting that it “encourages animal prints, flashy jewelry, [and] big fur coats.” She added, “It’s right there in the name: She’s a wife! She’s not a girl; she’s a woman. She’s confident in her sexuality. She puts ziti on the table. And she’s well aware of how the world works—and how to bend it to her advantage.”

    Product developer Kayla Trivieri, who coined the term “mob wife aesthetic” in a TikTok video, further explained, “It’s not about having the most expensive outfit. It’s an energy, an aura. It’s about being very forward and bold.”
