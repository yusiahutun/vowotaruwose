    Skid Row US Tour Starts In October

    FLORIDA (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Skid Row will be starting a US tour with special guests Kings X starting in late October through November. The tour will also feature Nashville Pussy in select cities. The tour is entitled the "Revolutions Per Minute" tour, the same name as their forthcoming album slated to be released at the end of October on SVP Records. More dates to be announced. – CelebrityAccess Staff Writers
