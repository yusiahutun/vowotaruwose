    Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce could start their overseas adventures thanks to Tay Tay’s Eras tour, as reports claim the NFL star could travel to watch her in concert.

    Ever since Taylor Swift was seen at Travis Kelce’s Kansas City Chiefs match, fans all over the world have wanted to know every little detail of their romantic life. While nothing has been confirmed by the celebrity couple, sources say that their relationship is pretty serious. They are so ‘in love,’ that Travis Kelce might travel all the way to Argentina to see Tay Tay perform for her thousands of fans at the River Plate’s Monumental stadium.

    Taylor Swift’s hold on Travis can be measured in miles

    Travis Kelce is reportedly traveling to Argentina all the way from Kansas, say sources. Travis hinted at his plans at the latest podcast episode of New Heights talking about his free weekend plans.

    The NFL celebrity said: “I might just say f**k it and go somewhere nice. My skin’s getting real pale, so I’ve got to go someplace sunny,” adding, “Some place south, closer to the equator.”

    According to a source, the 34-year-old sports star is looking forward to see Tay Tay in Argentina, but he will have to return in time for his NFL duties.

    “He has to be back on Sunday for practice on Monday,” the insider added.

    The distance between Kansas City and Argentina happens to be 5701 miles and fans are swooning over the fact that Tay Tay’s new beau will cover the distance to see the pop superstar in her true form.

    Travis Kelce misses out the first day of Eras tour

    Travis Kelce missed out on the first show of Taylor’s Argentina tour on Thursday. The star attended his Kansas City Chiefs teammate Patrick Mahomes’ 15 and the Mahomies Foundation gala.

    The star also got a shout-out at the gala for being the all-time leading receiver in Chiefs history. Fans shared the clip showing his humble attitude as Travis sported a dark blue slacks and a long-sleeved white button-down.

    Though Travis missed the first show, sources believe that he will fly out for the Friday or Saturday show of the Blank Space crooner.
