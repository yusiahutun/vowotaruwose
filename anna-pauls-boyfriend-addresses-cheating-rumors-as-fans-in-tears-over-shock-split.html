    Anna Paul’s boyfriend Glen has taken to Instagram to ‘clear up’ cheating rumors that have been flying around after the couple shockingly announced their eight-year relationship had come to an end over the weekend.

    The Only Fans and TikTok star and her boyfriend Glen had been together for eight years, since they were just 15 years old. Viewers have watched the pair grow together online, and are devastated at the ‘unexpected’ split.

    Anna Paul and Glen Thomson announce split after eight years

    The influencers broke hearts across the globe this weekend when they shockingly announced their eight-year relationship had come to an end via Anna’s TikTok page.

    In the 51-second video, she addressed the two had split as she said: “We still love each other, so much, and it was a mutual breakup.”

    She continued: “I love him so much, but we’re just not in a relationship anymore, we are now both single.”

    The star looked like she was holding back tears, as her now ex-boyfriend appeared at the end to give her a kiss on the forehead, proving they were still on good terms.

    In the caption she wrote: “Please be nice in the comments, it’s allllll love.”

    Anna Paul’s boyfriend Glen ‘clears up’ cheating rumors

    Although the pair stated they still had “so much love” for each other and the break-up was a mutual decision, rumors of infidelity spread around the internet. However, Glen has now taken to his Instagram story to clear these up, as he wrote:

    “There are soooo many conversations online about one of us cheating! I just wanted to clear up that neither of us cheated! We both aren’t like that!”

    On the next slide, he continued: “Breakups aren’t always toxic, trust me, it’s all love”.

    Fans can’t get over the breakup

    Anna and Glen’s news seems to have taken over TikTok and Twitter as fans are sharing their disatrught over the couple breaking up which has left them ‘in tears’.

    “ANNA PAUL AND GLENN BROKE UP NOOOOOO,” exclaimed one Twitter user.

    “Cannot believe Anna Paul and glen broke up im taking it worse than them,” wrote another.

    “I’m gonna cry, omg,” penned one fan.
