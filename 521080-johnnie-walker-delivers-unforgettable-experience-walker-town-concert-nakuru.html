    Johnie Walker Delivers an Unforgettable Experience at the Walker Town Concert in Nakuru

    08 Aug 2024, 00:00

    It was a one-of-a-kind experience for revelers as Johnnie Walker took the latest edition of the Walker Town Concert to the Rift Valley Sports Club in Nakuru.

    The event which took place on Saturday 6th May 2023 was headlined by Johnnie Walker’s lead brand ambassador in Kenya, Nyashinski, and Nigerian Afrobeats superstar Joeboy.

    The concert, part of Johnnie Walker’s Walker Town property, provided a unique experience with the best entertainment from local and international artists, engaging fun activities such as gaming, fashion, and art.

    Revelers were treated to an electrifying performance from Nyashinski who had the crowd on their feet with his chart-topping hits including Hapo Tu, Free, Mungu Pekee among others. Joeboy who performed ahead of Nyashinski also wowed the audience with his Afrobeats hits such as Body & Soul and Love Nwantiti, keeping the energy high throughout his performance.

    In addition to the headlining acts, the event also featured a line-up of renowned local and international DJs including DJ Joe Mfalme, DJ Pierra Makena, and DJ Protégé. The ever-energetic Gudaman and Kwambox ensured the energy was maintained throughout the night.

    We are thrilled with the success of the Walker Town Concert Nakuru edition. The event was truly unforgettable, with attendees enjoying the best entertainment, food, and drinks, all while experiencing the bold flavors of Johnnie Walker. We would like to thank our fans who came out to support us, and we promise to continue providing them with the very best of the Walker Town experience, said Johnnie Walker Marketing Manager, Josephine Katambo.

    The Nakuru edition was the latest in a series of Walker Town events that have taken place in several counties in Kenya since its launch in 2022.
