    What is Pochi La Biashara by Safaricom? Benefits and registration

    M-PESA merchants can receive payments from their customers through Pay Bill or Buy Goods and Services. However, one must register a separate business account to use the two services. A third option to receive payments without the rigorous registration steps is through Pochi La Biashara. It only requires merchants to use their registered M-PESA number to receive payments. But what are its benefits?

    Pochi La Biashara is a payment service from Safaricom’s M-PESA that allows small business owners to receive customer payments. Its unique selling point is that merchants can separate their business funds from personal funds or the money they already have in their M-PESA accounts. The service is ideal for business owners such as boda-boda operators, food vendors, shop retailers, and even hawkers.

    How does Safaricom Pochi La Biashara work?

    Pochi La Biashara works like a business account. Generally, it targets small business owners who have M-PESA accounts and want to receive money or make payments digitally. But instead of receiving money directly to their M-PESA accounts, it creates a separate account but on the same line.

    So, when a merchant receives money, it does not go to their M-PESA account. Instead, it goes directly to the Pochi La Biashara account. That way, a business owner can keep their personal funds separate from business funds. However, the merchant can always transfer their money to the M-PESA account.

    What are the benefits of Pochi La Biashara?

    Pochi La Biashara benefits merchants and customers in many ways. For example, customers can pay via M-PESA if they do not have cash. Also, customers can transact for free if they make payments of less than Ksh 100. Business owners benefit in the following ways:

    • Customers cannot reverse transactions unless the business owner approves the reversal.
    • The registration is straightforward and requires no paperwork.
    • Business owners can separate their business funds from M-PESA personal funds.
    • Easy access to a mini statement showing the top transactions of the day.
    • It also allows business owners to sell airtime from the money they receive from customers. In turn, they will receive a commission of 5% for the value of the airtime they sell.

    If a business owner has Fuliza (overdraft facility) from M-PESA, the service will not deduct any amount they receive from customers. Thus, the business account will remain intact. However, a deduction will occur once a merchant sends money from their Pochi wallet to an M-PESA account.

    How to join Pochi La Biashara

    Joining Pochi La Biashara is a straightforward process. First, you must be a registered M-PESA user to register. Then:

    • Dial USSD number *334#.
    • Select Pochi La Biashara.
    • Select Join Pochi.
    • Accept the Terms and Conditions.
    • Select the business category.

    After joining, M-PESA will create an account. Then, you will receive a message confirming you created an account. Finally, you will see a new menu that allows you to access your account and know your balance.

    How much can Pochi La Biashara hold?

    Pochi La Biashara works like a regular M-PESA account but with many differences. For example, the minimum amount that it can hold is Ksh 1, and the maximum is Ksh 300,000. Also, the daily transactional Pochi La Biashara limit is Ksh 300,000.

    What are the Pochi La Biashara charges?

    Pochi La Biashara charges money when customers send money from their M-PESA account to a merchant’s account. The transaction fees apply when making a payment to the business account. But once a business owner receives the money, they will not incur any charges.

    How to pay to Pochi La Biashara

    Customers can pay via a USSD code, M-PESA app, or STK menu. Paying using the STK menu is as follows:

    • Go to SIM Tool Kit app.
    • Select M-PESA.
    • Select Lipa Na M-PESA.
    • Select Pochi La Biashara.
    • Enter the merchant’s phone number.
    • Enter the amount.
    • Enter M-PESA PIN.

    M-PESA transaction charges will apply when sending money to Pochi La Biashara. A customer can also use the USSD code by doing the following:

    • Dial *334#.
    • Select Lipa Na M-PESA.
    • Select Pochi La Biashara.
    • Enter the merchant’s phone number.
    • Enter the amount.

    After providing the M-PESA pin, the merchant will receive funds in their Pochi La Biashara account.

    How to withdraw money from Pochi La Biashara

    You can withdraw funds from Pochi La Biashara in two ways. The first option is to withdraw from an agent, and the second is to send the money from the account to M-PESA.

    How to withdraw Pochi La Biashara funds from an M-PESA agent

    You can withdraw from an agent by doing the following:

    • Dial *334#.
    • Select Pochi La Biashara.
    • Select Withdraw Funds.
    • Select From Agent.
    • Enter the Agent number.
    • Enter the amount to withdraw.
    • Enter the M-PESA PIN to complete the transition.

    The withdrawal charges are the same M-PESA charges for withdrawing from an agent.

    How to send Pochi La Biashara funds to M-PESA

    The second option to access funds is to send them to an M-PESA number. Since Pochi La Biashara is a business account, you must send the money to your personal M-PESA account.

    • Dial *334#.
    • Select Pochi La Biashara.
    • Select Withdraw Funds.
    • Select From Business to Personal Account.
    • Enter the amount to withdraw.
    • Enter M-PESA PIN to complete the transaction.

    You can also access Pochi La Biashara funds by paying customers or other mobile numbers.

    How to opt out of Pochi La Biashara

    If you do not wish to receive payments using the payment service, you can opt-out anytime. To do this:

    • Dial *334#.
    • Select Pochi La Biashara.
    • Select My Account.
    • Select Leave this service.
    • Confirm the option.

    M-PESA will send a message to confirm the merchant has successfully opted out of the service.
