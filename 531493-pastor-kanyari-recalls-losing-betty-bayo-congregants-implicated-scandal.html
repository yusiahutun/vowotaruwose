    Renowned veteran preacher Pastor Victor Kanyari has recalled losing everything when he was implicated in a scandal. The pastor was allegedly accused of fraud in an investigative piece, where he was seen asking for money from his congregants to pray for their troubles.

    21 Feb 2024, 00:00
    • Pastor Victor Kanyari has recalled how a scandal he once faced ended his marriage and saw him lose his kids and congregants after the church lost faith in him
    • Kanyari revealed that it was very difficult for him after people who were close to him vanished, leaving him at the mercy of his parents
    • The man of God, however, maintained that refusing to give up in life is what made him sail through his tribulation

    Renowned veteran preacher Pastor Victor Kanyari has recalled losing everything when he was implicated in a scandal.

    Why Betty Bayo left Pastor Kanyari

    The pastor was allegedly accused of fraud in an investigative piece, where he was seen asking for money from his congregants to pray for their troubles.

    When the investigation report by Mohamed Ali and John Allan Namu aired, many people ditched his church, and he later parted ways with his wife.
