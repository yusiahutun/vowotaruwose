    19 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Drake has been caught up in a bunch of drama with just about every other rapper out there, and it looks like the stress may have aged him, fans seem to think.

    The Canadian music star was spotted at Nicki Minaj’s show in his hometown Toronto, and in one picture circulating around social media, Drake didn’t look the happiest he’s ever been, and some flans are blaming the ongoing ‘rap beef.’

    Drake spotted at Nicki Minaj’s concert – but he doesn’t look happy

    Despite having their ups and downs, like most friendships, long-term friends Drake and Nicki Minaj have always been there to support each other. And that’s exactly what the Shot For Me rapper was doing as Minaj brought her tour to Toronto.

    After wrapping up his mammoth tour with J.Cole, Drake, real name Aubrey Graham, was spotted by a fan in the crowd of the Pink Friday 2 Tour. However, in the pic circulating around X, formerly known as Twitter, he doesn’t seem to be having the best time right now.

    In short, the rapper recently released the diss track Push Ups, replying to stars Kendrick Lamar, Future, Metro Boomin, Rick Ross, and The Weeknd, to name a few.

    But it didn’t stop there. With Rick Ross, the ‘beef’ carried on via social media, with the Toronto-born rapper even involving screenshots of texts with his mom about the ongoings.

    Now, fans claim the 37-year-old looks like he’s aged since the feuds became public, as the new snap circulates.

    “He looks so old omgggg,” penned one shocked fan.

    “He honestly looks like he hasn’t been sleeping,” wrote one user. “Apology incoming.”

    “The rap beef got him looking so stressed!” exclaimed another.

    A fourth penned: “Dude is gettin’ old, time is flying.”

    The star first rose to fame in 2001, when he starred in the hit show Degrassi at just 14 years old. He then launched his music career by releasing his first mixtape five years later and has remained successfully under the spotlight since.

    The rapper gets a shout-out from Nicki

    Of course, Nicki had to make the rapper’s presence known in the crowd. In another video from the night, circulating online, she can be seen shouting out Aubrey.

    “Shout out to Drizzy Drake tonight, he in the building,” she can be heard announcing to the crowd, who erupt into cheers.

    The 41-year-old then begins to perform their latest collab Needle, however, reveals that the father of one said he wouldn’t be joining her on stage.

    He’s probably tired after his massive, It’s All A Blur tour, we’ll let him off…
